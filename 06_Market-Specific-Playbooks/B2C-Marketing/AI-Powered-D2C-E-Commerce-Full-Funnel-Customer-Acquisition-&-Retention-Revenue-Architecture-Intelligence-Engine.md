## Integration Tips

- **Meta Ads Manager:** Import campaign structure directly using Ads Manager bulk upload CSV. Use dynamic creative testing with the 6 creative concepts across 3 separate ad sets per campaign.
- **Klaviyo:** Create each flow using the Flow builder. Use conditional splits for SMS opt-in status (branch email-only vs. email+SMS paths). Set flow filters to exclude recent purchasers from welcome series.
- **Attentive / Postscript:** Configure SMS abandoned cart and win-back as separate SMS-native flows synced with Klaviyo via Zapier or native integration. Set quiet hours (9pm–9am local time).
- **Shopify + ReCharge / Skio:** Enable subscription offers on hero SKUs via product page subscription widget. Configure "subscribe and save" price compare at to show savings vs. one-time purchase.
- **Friendbuy / ReferralCandy:** Place referral widget on post-purchase confirmation page via Shopify Checkout Extension. Trigger referral invite email at day 21 post-purchase (after usage begins).
- **Triple Whale / Northbeam:** Configure pixel on all Shopify pages + Meta CAPI for server-side event matching. Create custom attribution window (7-day click, 1-day view) for true CAC visibility.
- **Notion Dashboard:** Auto-populate weekly KPI tracking table via Zapier connection to Klaviyo, Meta Ads API, and Shopify — no manual entry.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Meta ROAS is below target despite testing multiple creatives.**
Solution: Separate your campaigns into distinct cold (new audiences) and warm (retargeting) campaigns with separate budgets. Audit audience overlap using Audience Overlap tool — overlapping audiences cannibalize each other and inflate CPM. Shift 30% of cold budget to Google Shopping where purchase intent is explicit.

**Problem: Email flows are sending but conversion rate is under 3%.**
Solution: Audit send-time optimization — run A/B test on send time (Tuesday 10am vs Thursday 7pm vs Sunday 8pm in recipient timezone). Check your Klaviyo deliverability score; if below 85, warm up sending IP with a re-engagement purge of 6-month non-openers before next campaign send. Ensure welcome series offer (discount) is front-loaded in email 2, not buried in email 5.

**Problem: Subscription conversion rate is below 5%.**
Solution: Move the subscription offer earlier — add a subscription option on the product page (before purchase) with a visible "most popular" badge, not just in the post-purchase email sequence. Use Skio or ReCharge's "subscribe and save" widget directly on the PDP. A/B test a one-time-purchase vs. subscription as the default selection (subscription pre-selected with opt-out to one-time).
