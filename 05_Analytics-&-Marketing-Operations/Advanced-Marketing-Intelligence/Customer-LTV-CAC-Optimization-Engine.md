# Customer LTV & CAC Optimization Engine - AI-Powered Lifetime Value and Acquisition Cost Intelligence

**Difficulty:** Advanced | **Time:** 25 min | **Tags:** analytics, ltv, cac, retention, unit-economics, b2b, saas, d2c, growth, data-driven

## Overview
This prompt ingests your cohort, channel, and retention data to compute Customer Lifetime Value (LTV) and Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) by segment, model LTV:CAC ratios by channel, calculate payback periods, and generate a prioritized action plan to improve unit economics. Use it quarterly to guide budget allocation, or immediately before a board meeting when you need to defend growth efficiency.

## Quick Copy-Paste Version

You are a growth finance analyst and marketing economist. I need a complete LTV:CAC analysis and unit economics optimization plan for my business.

My business context:
- Business model: [B2B SaaS subscription / D2C ecommerce / marketplace / transactional]
- Customer segment focus: [SMB / Mid-Market / Enterprise / Consumer]
- Average contract value or order value: $[X] per [month/year/order]
- Gross margin: [X]%

Acquisition data (last 12 months):
- Total new customers acquired: [X]
- Total marketing + sales spend: $[X]
- Blended CAC (total spend / new customers): $[X]

Channel-level CAC breakdown:
- Paid search: $[X] spent, [X] customers
- Paid social: $[X] spent, [X] customers
- SEO/Content: $[X] spent, [X] customers
- Outbound/SDR: $[X] spent, [X] customers
- Events/webinars: $[X] spent, [X] customers
- Partner/referral: $[X] spent, [X] customers

Retention data:
- Monthly churn rate: [X]% OR Annual churn rate: [X]%
- Average customer lifespan (if known): [X] months
- Net Revenue Retention (NRR): [X]%
- Average expansion revenue per retained customer per year: $[X]

Please deliver:
1. LTV calculation by segment with the formula and assumptions made explicit
2. LTV:CAC ratio by channel — ranked from most to least efficient
3. CAC payback period by channel (months to recoup acquisition cost)
4. The top 3 levers to improve LTV (ranked by dollar impact)
5. The top 3 levers to reduce CAC (ranked by dollar impact)
6. A budget reallocation recommendation based on channel LTV:CAC ratios
7. A 90-day action plan to improve overall unit economics
