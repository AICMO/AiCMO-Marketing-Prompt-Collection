## Integration Tips

**HubSpot:**
- Create custom contact/deal property `dark_funnel_first_touch` (multi-select checkbox)
- Add attribution survey to demo confirmation email as required form
- Build report: "Self-Reported Attribution Distribution" by month to track dark funnel data quality
- Workflow: if `dark_funnel_first_touch` includes "Podcast," auto-tag deal with podcast campaign ID

**Salesforce:**
- Add `Dark_Funnel_Source__c` picklist field to Lead and Opportunity objects
- Create dashboard component: "Influenced vs. Tracked Pipeline" using combined fields
- Campaign influence model: create "Community Influence" and "Podcast Influence" campaign types for soft-touch attribution

**Slack/Make/Zapier:**
- Automate: when Slack community mentions "[company name]," log to Google Sheet with timestamp, channel, poster
- Weekly digest: aggregate community mentions + brand search volume + AI citation audit into Slack ops channel
- Trigger: if spike in "Direct/None" traffic > 30%, alert marketing ops team to investigate

**Google Search Console + Looker Studio:**
- Track branded search volume as dark funnel proxy — rising brand searches indicate increasing awareness
- Build "Brand Health Score" = (branded search volume × brand search CTR) / (all traffic benchmark)
- Overlay pipeline data on brand search trend to visualize correlation

**Perplexity/ChatGPT AI Search Audit:**
- Weekly prompt test: "What are the best [category] tools for [ICP]?" — log whether your brand appears
- Track: mention position, context, sentiment, and linked sources
- Feed results into brand dashboard as "AI Search Visibility Score"

## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** Survey response rates are too low to draw conclusions (below 40%)
**Solution:** Move the attribution question into the sales discovery call as a verbal question ("Before we jump in — how'd you first hear about us?"), train SDRs to log it in CRM. Incentivize completion by making it part of the demo confirmation flow, not a follow-up email. A/B test: embed directly in the calendar booking form vs. post-demo email.

**Problem:** Executives don't trust "estimated" pipeline numbers and demand hard proof
**Solution:** Start with self-reported data, which is first-party and defensible. Present it as "here's what prospects tell us" rather than a model. Run a cohort study comparing 90-day close rates for "community-sourced" self-reported deals vs. other sources — if community-sourced deals close at 2× the rate, that's hard evidence. Use "correlation, not causation" framing, and reference Forrester/SiriusDecisions benchmarks to show this gap is industry-wide.

**Problem:** Can't track which community member becomes a lead — no PII linkage
**Solution:** Use IP-level intent data tools (6sense, Demandbase, Bombora) to see which accounts are researching your category even before form fills. Cross-reference: accounts active in communities (identified by community membership list) that show up in intent data = high-confidence dark funnel signal. Alternatively, create community-exclusive offers (invite-only webinars, early access programs) with unique registration links to create the PII link.
