## Overview
This prompt designs a complete interactive content program — ROI calculators, maturity assessments, diagnostic tools, and benchmark quizzes — that qualifies buyers through self-service while generating pipeline-ready leads with embedded intent signals. Use it when your gated content conversion rates have plateaued, your MQLs lack quality context, or you need a self-serve demand capture engine that works 24/7 without SDR involvement.

## Integration Tips

- **HubSpot**: Use HubSpot's form embed with hidden fields to pass calculator inputs and result scores into contact properties. Map maturity tiers to custom contact properties and build enrollment workflows that trigger nurture sequences based on tier.
- **Salesforce**: Create a custom object "Interactive Content Completion" linked to Lead/Contact. Store: asset name, completion date, score, tier, and calculated result values. Add to lead view for SDRs so they can see results before calling.
- **Outgrow / Typeform**: Both natively integrate with HubSpot and Salesforce. Outgrow has native ROI calculator templates; Typeform Logic Jumps handle branching diagnostic paths. Use Zapier or Make.com to push results to Slack for real-time SDR notifications.
- **Customer.io / Marketo**: Trigger behavioral email sequences based on completion events. Pass score variables into email personalization tokens so "Your ROI: $X" appears dynamically in subject lines and body copy.
- **Slack**: Use Zapier to create a #hot-leads channel that auto-posts whenever a calculator completion generates >400% ROI or a maturity assessment scores in Tier 3. Include buyer name, company, LinkedIn URL, and key inputs.
- **Webflow**: Embed Typeform or Outgrow iframes directly in Webflow pages. Use Webflow's built-in analytics to measure traffic-to-embed-start rate and identify which pages drive highest calculator engagement.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** Completion rate is below 40% — users start the assessment but abandon.
**Solution:** The most common cause is question fatigue or friction at question 4–6. Audit your question sequence: (1) Move the most engaging/provocative question to position 1–2 to hook users early. (2) Add a progress bar that shows "3 questions left" (not "60% complete" — finite framing outperforms percentage). (3) Remove any open-text fields; replace all free-form inputs with sliders or multiple choice. (4) Test a shorter 6-question version against the full 10-question version.

**Problem:** Gate conversion rate is below 20% — users complete the tool but don't submit contact info.
**Solution:** This usually means results are visible before the gate, eliminating incentive to convert. Solutions: (1) Show a "teaser" result (e.g., "Your ROI is somewhere between $200k–$800k") and gate the precise figure. (2) Add social proof at the gate: "Join 3,400 RevOps leaders who've benchmarked their program." (3) Reduce gate friction to email-only first, then progressive profile with role/company size on the thank-you page. (4) Test a "share your results" CTA that requires email to generate a shareable PDF report.

**Problem:** Sales team isn't using the calculator in outbound — it's only generating inbound leads.
**Solution:** Build an SDR-first version: a 3-input "quick calculator" that takes only 30 seconds and is designed to be run during a cold call or embedded in a cold email ("I built a quick calculator that estimates how much your team might be spending on manual reporting — want me to share it? Takes 45 seconds."). Create a Salesforce button that lets AEs run the calculator for a prospect live on a discovery call and attach the result to the opportunity.
