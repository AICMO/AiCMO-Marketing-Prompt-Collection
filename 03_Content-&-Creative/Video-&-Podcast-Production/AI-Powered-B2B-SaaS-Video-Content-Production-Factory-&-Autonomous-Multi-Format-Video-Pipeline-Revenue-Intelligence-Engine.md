## Troubleshooting

**Problem: AI avatar lip-sync looks robotic or uncanny valley**
- Fix: Use HeyGen's "instant avatar" feature with a real video recording rather than photo-based avatars; ensure script uses short, punchy sentences (AI avatars handle 12-15 word sentences better than long complex ones); avoid scripts with lots of technical jargon that creates unusual mouth shapes. For enterprise audiences, consider filming a real spokesperson 1x/month for hero content and using AI avatars only for high-volume educational content.

**Problem: Video views are high but demo requests aren't converting from video CTAs**
- Fix: Audit where your CTA appears in the video — bottom-of-funnel CTAs (book demo) require a viewer who is already in decision mode; if most traffic is awareness-stage (YouTube organic), replace demo CTAs with softer conversion offers (download the guide, watch the full demo playlist, join the webinar). Add a Wistia or Vidyard email gate 60% through longer videos so you capture the lead before they bounce. A/B test CTA placement: mid-video (at highest attention point) vs. end-of-video.

**Problem: The sales team isn't adopting personalized video — they find it too time-consuming**
- Fix: Reduce friction to under 60 seconds of effort per video. Pre-record a 45-second product highlight clip that handles the "what we do" automatically — SDR only needs to record a 15-second personal intro in Loom. Create a 1-click video send button inside Salesloft/Outreach using Vidyard's native integration. Run a 2-week competition: SDR who sends the most personalized videos and books the most meetings wins a $200 gift card. Share video-vs-text open rate comparisons weekly to maintain motivation.
