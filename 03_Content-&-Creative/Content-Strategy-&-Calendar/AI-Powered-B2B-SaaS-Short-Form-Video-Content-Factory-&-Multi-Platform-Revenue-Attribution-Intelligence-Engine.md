## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Videos get views but zero pipeline influence**
Solution: Your CTA architecture is wrong. Awareness-stage videos should drive profile visits and follows, not demo requests. Add a content upgrade CTA ("comment X for template") to build your email list. Reserve hard CTAs (book demo, start trial) exclusively for Decision-stage videos (proof stories, product demos). Audit your video-to-landing-page journey — every friction point between watch and form fill kills attribution.

**Problem: Production is taking 2-3 weeks per video and the calendar keeps slipping**
Solution: You're not batching. The biggest productivity unlock in video is filming 8-12 scripts in one session. Standardize your setup (one background, one lighting configuration, one outfit per batch session) to eliminate decision fatigue. Invest 1 hour in a one-time teleprompter setup ($80 hardware or free app like PromptSmart). Scripts should be written and approved at least 5 business days before filming.

**Problem: Hook retention is below 40% — people are dropping off in the first 3 seconds**
Solution: Your hooks are too soft. Analyze your bottom-performing videos: are you starting with "Hi, I'm [Name]" or "Today I want to talk about..."? These are death sentences on short-form. Replace with pattern interrupts: a surprising statistic, a contrarian statement, a direct "you're doing X wrong" opener, or a visual prop. A/B test 2 hooks on the same topic — same video body, different first 3 seconds. The hook is 80% of your video's performance ceiling.
