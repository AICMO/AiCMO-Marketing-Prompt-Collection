## Overview
Designs and operationalizes a systematic AI-powered customer testimonial video engine — from customer selection scoring and interview preparation through production briefs, multi-channel distribution architecture, and pipeline revenue attribution. Use when you need to build or scale a proof-based selling system that turns customer outcomes into a compounding revenue asset.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Customer agrees but goes dark before filming — the "commitment drop-off"**
Solution: Reduce friction immediately after agreement. Within 24 hours of saying yes, send a 3-step "everything is handled" email: (1) automated calendar link for a 20-min logistics call, (2) the question list so they can prepare without pressure, (3) a sample finished video so they know what it will look and feel like. The biggest drop-off driver is uncertainty — remove it proactively.

**Problem: Videos go into the sales asset library but reps don't use them**
Solution: Usage is a training + workflow problem, not a content problem. Fix both: (1) Add 3 video links directly into your CRM deal stage templates so they appear as "suggested next actions" at the right deal stage — removing the decision of which video to use. (2) In the next sales training or SKO, have the #1 revenue AE walk through a deal they won using video — peer modeling converts faster than manager mandates.

**Problem: Customers won't approve specific metrics for public use**
Solution: Build a "tiered disclosure" system into your video permission form: Level 1 (most customers agree) = percentage improvements ("reduced time by 60%"); Level 2 (some customers agree) = absolute dollar figures with ranges ("saved between $800K and $1.5M"); Level 3 (rare) = exact named metrics. Most customers who hesitate on Level 3 will approve Level 1. Script your interview questions to capture both — then let legal approval determine which version goes public.
