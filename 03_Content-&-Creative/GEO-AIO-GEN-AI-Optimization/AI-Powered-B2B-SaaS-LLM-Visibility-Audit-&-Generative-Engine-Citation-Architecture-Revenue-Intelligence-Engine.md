## Integration Tips

**Perplexity API + Google Sheets:** Automate weekly query testing by sending your 10 core prompts to Perplexity's API and logging responses to a Google Sheet. A Zapier workflow can score mentions and alert your Slack channel when a competitor gains or loses share.

**G2 + HubSpot:** Connect G2 review alerts to HubSpot workflows. When a new review mentions a specific use case (e.g., "feature adoption"), tag it and route the customer for a case study conversation.

**Notion Content Database:** Track every citation asset as a Notion database record with fields for: content type, publication date, target queries, citation likelihood score, and observed LLM mentions. Link to the content and update monthly.

**Clearscope or Surfer SEO:** Use these tools to optimize citation assets for search AND LLM discoverability — high-search-ranking content gets indexed by LLM training crawls more frequently.

**Ahrefs/Semrush + Manual LLM Testing:** Combine traditional backlink and ranking data with weekly LLM query testing. Leading SEO signals (domain authority growth, featured snippets) often predict LLM citation improvements 60-90 days ahead.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: We run the audit and we're invisible across all 4 LLM platforms.**
Fix: This means your brand lacks sufficient indexed signal volume — not a content quality problem, a content quantity and distribution problem. Prioritize G2/Capterra reviews first (fastest path to structured third-party signal), then publish one comparison page targeting your top competitor. Both take under 30 days. Don't write more blog posts yet — LLMs already have more content than they need; they need proof signals.

**Problem: We appear in Perplexity but never in ChatGPT-4o.**
Fix: Perplexity uses real-time RAG (retrieval-augmented generation) and will cite anything well-indexed with current search signals. ChatGPT-4o relies more on training data cutoffs and uses broader authority signals. To improve ChatGPT citations: focus on high-DA editorial placements (TechCrunch, G2 editorial, Forbes, industry trade publications), analyst mentions (Gartner, Forrester, IDC), and Wikipedia-style factual mentions. Your content needs to appear in sources that were in ChatGPT's training corpus.

**Problem: We improved our mention rate on 5 queries but saw no increase in AI-referred traffic.**
Fix: Most LLM conversations don't result in immediate clicks — they result in brand recall that influences a search 48-72 hours later. Track branded search volume in Google Search Console alongside LLM mention rate. A rising branded search trend after you improve LLM visibility is confirmation the strategy is working, even before AI-referred UTMs show up in analytics.
