## Integration Tips

- **Impact.com / PartnerStack:** Use the "custom commission rules" feature to implement the two-gate payout logic. Create an automated webhook from HubSpot that triggers Gate 2 payout when the "Customer Health: Activated" property is set to true.
- **HubSpot CRM:** Build an "Affiliate Program" pipeline view with custom lifecycle stages: Affiliate-Referred > MQL > SQL (Gate 1 trigger) > Opportunity > Closed-Won > Activated (Gate 2 trigger). Set up automated workflows that notify the affiliate platform at each gate.
- **Salesforce + Revenue Intelligence:** If using Clearbit or 6sense, enrich all affiliate-referred leads at entry and immediately flag any leads outside your ICP — send those back to the affiliate with a "qualification coaching" note to reduce future mis-referrals.
- **Slack / Email Digest:** Build a weekly affiliate performance digest (auto-generated from Looker Studio or HubSpot reporting) that shows Gate 1 conversions, Gate 2 confirmations, and commission liability vs. pipeline value. Send to VP Marketing and RevOps every Monday.
- **Notion / Google Sheets:** Maintain a Platinum Partner dossier for your top 10 affiliates — include their audience profile, content formats that convert best, co-marketing campaign history, and next action items. Update after every partner call.
- **Zapier / Make:** Automate the dormant re-engagement sequence trigger: when an affiliate's "last referral date" in your affiliate platform exceeds 60 days, fire a Zap that enrolls them in the HubSpot re-engagement workflow and creates a follow-up task for the partner manager.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Affiliates revolt against the new two-gate commission structure, threatening to leave or promote competitors.**
Solution: Run a "grandfather" transition period of 90 days where existing affiliates earn their current flat rate AND the new quality bonus tier simultaneously. After 90 days, the new structure is permanent. Present the change as "earning more for the same qualified traffic" — the $900 total potential payout is higher than the current $800 for affiliates with good SQL rates. Frame the clawback policy around "protecting your commission earnings from bad-fit customers" rather than "penalizing low-quality referrals."

**Problem: The two-gate model creates commission payment delays that damage affiliate trust.**
Solution: Implement Gate 1 as a rapid-release payment (within 5 business days of SQL confirmation, not end-of-month batch). Use Impact.com's "instant payment" feature for Gate 1 amounts. The speed of Gate 1 payment compensates psychologically for the delayed Gate 2 payout. Create a real-time affiliate dashboard showing pending Gate 2 payouts as "earnings in escrow."

**Problem: Co-marketing co-investments with Elite affiliates aren't generating measurable pipeline.**
Solution: Add a mutual accountability clause to every co-marketing agreement: both parties commit to defined deliverables (e.g., "Affiliate promotes to minimum 5,000 email subscribers; Clearline provides $2,000 in production support") and both share a post-campaign dashboard. If co-marketing ROI doesn't exceed 3:1 pipeline-to-investment within 60 days, the co-marketing level resets to the previous tier and both parties assess what failed.
