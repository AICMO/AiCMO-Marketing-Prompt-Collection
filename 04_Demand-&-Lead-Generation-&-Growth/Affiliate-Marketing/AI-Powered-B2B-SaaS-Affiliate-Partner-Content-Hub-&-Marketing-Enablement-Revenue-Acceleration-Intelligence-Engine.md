## Integration Tips

- **PartnerStack / Impact.com**: Export the Tiered Incentive Playbook structure directly into milestone reward rules; map commission tiers to certification levels in the LMS module
- **Notion / Confluence**: Build the Partner Content Hub as a Notion database — filter swipe copy by channel, variation, and ICP segment; gate Levels 2-3 of training behind commission tier upgrades

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Affiliates sign up but never post or refer anyone**
High signup / low activation is almost always a motivation or clarity problem. Check: (1) Is the commission payout timeline clear? Partners disengage when they can't see quick wins. Consider a micro-bonus for first referral within 14 days. (2) Is the swipe copy TOO polished? Some affiliates feel the copy doesn't sound like them and don't know how to adapt it. Add a section titled "How to make this sound like you" with 3 simple rewriting prompts. (3) Are you communicating monthly via a partner newsletter? Silence = abandonment.

**Problem: Affiliates are referring low-quality leads (poor ICP fit)**
This means the "Who to Refer and Who NOT to Refer" guidance needs to be more specific. Rewrite the ICP description with disqualifying attributes (company size, tech stack, team structure) that signal a bad fit — not just qualifying ones. Add real examples of "great referral" vs. "not a fit" as brief scenarios. Optionally, add a pre-qualification checklist affiliates can send to contacts before submitting them as referrals.

**Problem: Good traffic from affiliate links but low demo/trial conversion**
The affiliate's audience expects one thing from their content; your landing page delivers another. Audit for message mismatch: what ICP angle does the affiliate lead with (pain? ROI? peer recommendation?) vs. what does your landing page headline emphasize? Build landing page variants matching each angle and give affiliates the variant UTM link that matches their content style.
