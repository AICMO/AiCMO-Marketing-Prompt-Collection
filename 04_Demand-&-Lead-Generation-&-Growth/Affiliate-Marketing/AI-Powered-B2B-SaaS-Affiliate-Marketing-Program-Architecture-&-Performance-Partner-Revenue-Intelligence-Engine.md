## Overview

Designs and launches a full-stack affiliate marketing program for B2B SaaS companies—recruiting content creators, software review sites, industry influencers, and niche publishers who drive qualified trials and pipeline in exchange for performance-based commissions. Use this when building a new affiliate channel from scratch, restructuring an underperforming program, or scaling affiliate to 15–25% of new ARR without proportional headcount.

## Success Metrics

**Program Health Indicators (review monthly):**
- Number of active affiliates (defined as: at least 1 conversion in the past 30 days)
- Affiliate-sourced MRR as % of total new MRR (target: 15–25% at program maturity)
- Average affiliate click-to-trial conversion rate (benchmark: 2–5% for B2B SaaS)
- Average trial-to-paid conversion rate for affiliate traffic vs. other channels (affiliate should be 10–20% higher than paid ads if affiliate targeting is quality)
- Affiliate CAC vs. paid channel CAC (affiliate should be 30–50% lower once program matures)
- 90-day customer retention rate for affiliate-sourced customers (should match or exceed overall retention)
- Commission-to-revenue ratio (target: under 20% of affiliate-sourced ARR)
- Affiliate churn rate (% of active affiliates who become dormant each month; target: under 10%/month)

**Green Flags (program is working):**
- Affiliates are generating content without being prompted
- Inbound affiliate applications increasing month-over-month
- Affiliate-sourced customers have equal or better retention than other acquisition channels
- Top affiliates are proactively requesting new content to promote

**Red Flags (program needs intervention):**
- High click volume, low trial conversion (<1%): landing page or offer problem
- High trial, low paid conversion: wrong audience being sent by affiliates
- High commission payouts, low retained revenue: affiliate fraud or ICP mismatch
- Affiliate content not appearing in organic search for review keywords: SEO/content quality issue

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: High click volume but near-zero trial conversions (under 0.5%)**
Solution: The affiliate landing page is losing people. Audit the page against Deliverable 5's brief—check if you're sending affiliates to your homepage instead of a dedicated landing page, whether the CTA is buried, or whether the page load speed is over 3 seconds on mobile. Also verify the affiliate's tracking link isn't broken. Install Hotjar or Microsoft Clarity on the landing page to watch session recordings from affiliate traffic.

**Problem: Affiliates sign up but never promote**
Solution: Your onboarding sequence (Deliverable 4) isn't creating enough momentum. The most common cause is that affiliates don't have a natural "slot" to insert your promotion into their existing content. Audit your top 5 inactive affiliates' recent content and write them a personalized "here's exactly how I'd promote this in your format" email with a draft script or outline written for their specific style. Conversion rate on this personalized approach is typically 40–60% vs. <10% for generic follow-ups.

**Problem: Affiliate-sourced customers are churning at 2× the rate of other channels**
Solution: You have an ICP mismatch—affiliates are sending you traffic that looks like conversions but isn't your target buyer. Analyze the company size, role, and use case of churned affiliate customers vs. retained ones. If a specific affiliate is driving most churn, have a direct conversation about their audience demographics and consider adjusting their commission to be retention-gated (pay full commission only when the customer reaches 90 days). Introduce quality scoring per affiliate into your scorecard (Deliverable 6).
