### Messaging Guardrails

**APPROVED messages (use in your own words):**
- "[Product] helps [ICP role] [primary outcome] faster/easier/cheaper"
- The specific features you actually use and find valuable
- Honest comparisons to tools you've personally used
- Your actual results (with appropriate context)

**PROHIBITED claims (never use):**
- Revenue guarantees or income claims ("will increase revenue by X%")
- Claims you cannot personally verify from your own use
- Comparative claims that disparage competitors by name without factual basis
- Feature claims that are not currently in the product
- Pricing that may have changed since your last verification

## SECTION 4: CONTENT COMPLIANCE & BRAND SAFETY SYSTEM

### Pre-Publication Review Workflow

**Step 1 — Creator Submission (automated):**
Creator uploads content link or file to affiliate portal before publishing. System automatically scans for:
- Presence of FTC/ASA disclosure language (keyword scan: "affiliate," "commission," "sponsored," "#ad," "#paid")
- Brand name spelling and trademark usage
- Prohibited claim categories (AI scan using keyword and sentiment rules)
- Competitor mentions that may violate partnership agreement

**Step 2 — Automated Compliance Score (within 1 hour):**
- Green (90–100): Auto-approved; creator can publish immediately
- Yellow (70–89): Auto-approved with flagged items sent to creator for optional correction
- Red (below 70): Held for manual review; creator notified within 4 hours

**Step 3 — Manual Review (for Red-flagged content, within 48 hours):**
Partner Manager reviews flagged content and takes one of three actions:
- Approve with feedback (creator must note feedback for future content)
- Request revision (specific change required before approval)
- Reject (content cannot be published; explanation provided)

**Compliance Checklist for Creators (pre-submission self-check):**

- [ ] I have included clear disclosure that I earn a commission ("affiliate link," "#ad," or equivalent)
- [ ] The disclosure appears BEFORE the affiliate link, not after
- [ ] I have not made income or revenue guarantees I cannot personally verify
- [ ] I have checked that all pricing information I mention is current (verified at [pricing page URL])
- [ ] I have not made claims about features that are not currently live in the product
- [ ] I have used the product myself before recommending it in this content
- [ ] I have not disparaged any competitor by name based on unverified claims

**Creator Strike and Termination Policy:**

| Violation | Consequence |
|-----------|-------------|
| Missing disclosure on first piece | Warning + mandatory compliance training |
| Missing disclosure on second piece | 30-day commission hold; required re-certification |
| False product claims (unintentional) | Immediate content removal request; commission hold until resolved |
| False product claims (intentional/repeated) | Program termination; commission forfeiture; legal review if necessary |
| Negative competitive content violating partnership agreement | 24-hour cure period; termination if not corrected |

## SECTION 6: PERFORMANCE ANALYTICS & PROGRAM OPTIMIZATION ENGINE

### 10 Core KPIs for Creator Affiliate Program

| KPI | Formula | Benchmark Target | Measurement Cadence |
|-----|---------|-----------------|---------------------|
| Creator Conversion Rate | Paid conversions ÷ Clicks generated | 3–8% (varies by price point) | Weekly per creator |
| Affiliate-Sourced Customer LTV (12-month) | Average 12-month revenue from affiliate customers | 25%+ above blended company LTV | Monthly cohort |
| Program ROAS | Total revenue from affiliate customers ÷ Total program cost | 4:1 minimum; 8:1 target | Monthly |
| Creator Activation Rate | Creators who published ≥1 piece in last 30 days ÷ Total enrolled creators | 60%+ | Monthly |
| Content Compliance Rate | Content pieces passing compliance review without revision ÷ Total submissions | 85%+ | Monthly |
| Commission Efficiency | Total commissions paid ÷ Total affiliate-sourced revenue | Below [X]% of LTV | Monthly |
| Creator Churn Rate | Creators who exited program in quarter ÷ Total active at quarter start | Below 15% | Quarterly |
| LTV:CAC Ratio (Affiliate Channel) | Average 12-month LTV ÷ Average commission paid per customer | 3:1+ minimum | Quarterly |
| Content-to-Trial Conversion Rate | Trials from affiliate source ÷ Content pieces published that period | Track trending improvement | Monthly |
| Program ARR Growth | Affiliate-sourced ARR this quarter vs. last quarter | 15%+ QoQ growth in Year 1 | Quarterly |

### Monthly Creator Performance Review Cadence

**Week 1 (First week of month):**
- Pull previous month performance report from affiliate platform
- Identify top 20% of creators by revenue generated — flag for retainer upgrade offers
- Identify bottom 20% of creators who have been active 60+ days with zero conversions — schedule performance calls
- Review content compliance trends — update guardrails if specific violation types are increasing

**Week 2:**
- Publish monthly creator newsletter: top content pieces of the month (social proof for other creators), platform-specific performance insights, product updates and talking points for next month, featured creator spotlight

**Week 3:**
- Quarterly bonus calculation (if month = end of quarter): process and pay bonuses
- New creator pipeline: review applications received in the prior month using scoring rubric; send offers to qualified applicants

**Week 4:**
- Commission processing for previous month conversions
- Update Creator Dashboard with new performance data
- Identify content formats driving highest conversion rates — create new content briefs based on what's working

### Optimization Levers by Problem

| Problem Signal | Root Cause Diagnosis | Optimization Action |
|---------------|---------------------|---------------------|
| High clicks, low trial rate | Landing page or product offer mismatch | A/B test landing pages by creator audience segment; add creator-specific social proof |
| High trial rate, low paid conversion | Product-audience fit issue or trial activation failure | Review which creators' audiences have highest trial-to-paid CVR; refocus recruitment on those ICP segments |
| High conversion rate, low clicks | Creator content is reaching too small an audience | Invest in scaling highest-performing creators to Mid tier with retainer; amplify their content via paid social |
| Low creator content output | Enablement gap or lack of inspiration | Deploy monthly content brief with data-backed topic recommendations; create content inspiration feed from customer use cases |
| High creator churn | Commission structure not competitive or management overhead too high | Audit competitor affiliate program terms; add automated performance reporting to reduce manual work per creator |

## Troubleshooting

**Issue: Creators are generating clicks but almost no trial signups — conversion rate below 1%**
Solution: The traffic quality is likely fine — the landing page experience is creating friction. Creator-referred visitors arrive with high intent and social proof already applied; if your landing page doesn't immediately match the specific context from the content they just consumed (e.g., creator mentioned "DTC brands using attribution tools" and your landing page shows generic messaging), conversion drops dramatically. Build creator-specific landing pages or at minimum use URL parameters to dynamically change the headline and first paragraph based on creator segment. Test personalized landing pages that mirror the creator's content framing — typical conversion lift: 40–80%.

**Issue: Creator content quality is declining after Month 3 — content sounds increasingly scripted and promotional**
Solution: This is creator burnout from over-reliance on your messaging guidelines. Creators who felt free to speak in their natural voice in Month 1 start defaulting to approved messaging language when they've run out of genuine experiences to share. The fix: schedule quarterly "product discovery" sessions where creators get early access to new features, beta programs, and customer stories — giving them genuinely new material to discuss. Also introduce "creator questions" into your product team feedback loop so creators can ask the product team directly and share the authentic answers. Real information is the best antidote to scripted content.

**Issue: Top-performing creators are being poached by competitors with higher commission offers**
Solution: Commission rate alone rarely wins the loyalty war against a well-structured creator relationship. Audit your non-financial creator value: Are they getting early access to new features? Do they have a direct line to the product team? Are they featured in your own marketing (co-marketing that builds their audience)? Are their content pieces amplified via your paid social budget? Competitors can match a commission rate in 24 hours — they cannot match a 6-month relationship built on genuine partnership. For your top 10% of creators, implement a "founding creator" program with irreplaceable benefits: lifetime commission rate lock, co-marketing credits, speaking slots at your user conference, and quarterly executive access.
