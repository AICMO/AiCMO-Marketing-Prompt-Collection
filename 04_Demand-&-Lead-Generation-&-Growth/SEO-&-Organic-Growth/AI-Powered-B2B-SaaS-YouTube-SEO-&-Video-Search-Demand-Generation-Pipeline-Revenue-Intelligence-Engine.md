## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Videos rank well on YouTube Search but generate zero demo clicks**
- Diagnosis: The video content attracts the wrong audience (too broad/TOFU) or the CTA architecture is weak (no clear next step, buried links)
- Fix: Audit your top-10-performing videos and classify each by intent tier. If most views come from TOFU keywords, shift production to BOFU topics. Add/update pinned comments with specific demo CTAs. Test moving the in-video CTA earlier (to the 25% mark instead of 30%) for highly intent-driven videos like competitor comparisons.

**Problem: Algorithm isn't recommending your videos — Suggested Video section shows 0 traffic**
- Diagnosis: Insufficient watch time or your channel lacks topical authority (too many disconnected topics)
- Fix: Audit your video topics for coherence — if you have videos on 6 different subtopics, the algorithm doesn't know what your channel is "about." Consolidate into 3–4 tightly clustered topic areas for the next 90 days. Improve average view duration by re-editing video intros (the first 30 seconds are the retention kill zone in B2B — lead with the specific outcome, not an intro). Create 5 "session bait" videos that explicitly end with "watch this next" pointing to another playlist video.

**Problem: YouTube leads convert at a much lower rate to demos than other inbound sources**
- Diagnosis: YouTube is attracting educational intent (TOFU/MOFU) leads, not buying intent (BOFU) leads — or your sales team doesn't know how to handle YouTube-educated leads who "already know the basics"
- Fix: Analyze which specific videos are sourcing the underperforming leads (likely TOFU content). Shift production priority to BOFU videos (competitor comparisons, pricing transparency, demo walkthroughs) which attract buyers already in evaluation mode. Brief your SDR/AE team: YouTube-sourced leads often respond better to "I saw you watched our video on [topic] — what questions came up for your specific situation?" than generic outreach. Add qualification questions to your lead magnet forms: "Are you currently evaluating [category] solutions?" captures buying intent signal at the top of funnel.
