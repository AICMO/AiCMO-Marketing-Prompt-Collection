## Success Metrics

**Program-Level KPIs (Track Monthly):**
- Affiliate recruitment rate: 15–25 qualified new affiliates/month
- 30-day activation rate: 60%+ of recruited affiliates make first promotion within 30 days of joining
- Click-to-trial conversion: 3–7% (below 3% = landing page problem; above 7% = exceptional audience fit)
- Trial-to-paid conversion: 20–35% (reflects ICP match quality)
- Revenue concentration risk: No single affiliate > 30% of program revenue
- Partner CAC: Below 25% of ACV ($6,000 cap on $24K ACV)
- Revenue per active affiliate: $2,000+ ARR per month at program maturity (12 months in)
- Affiliate churn: Less than 20%/year active-to-inactive degradation

**Quality Validation:**
- ICP match score: Affiliate-referred leads should score 80%+ on ICP criteria vs. 75% benchmark for direct leads
- Demo-to-close rate: Should be within 10% of direct channel (affiliate-referred leads shouldn't require more selling)
- Customer churn: Affiliate-sourced customer 12-month retention should be within 5% of direct-acquired cohort

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Affiliates sign up, get access, and then never promote**
Solution: The activation failure is almost always caused by too much friction at the "first promotion" moment. Implement a mandatory Quick Win challenge: affiliates must post one pre-written social caption within 7 days of onboarding. Provide the exact caption — zero creativity required. Data shows affiliates who complete this Quick Win activate at 3× the rate of those who don't. Pair with a 14-day inactivity trigger that auto-sends a "here's what top affiliates did in week one" email with a copy-paste LinkedIn post attached.

**Problem: High click volume but near-zero trial conversions**
Solution: Traffic quality is fine but there's a landing page mismatch — the affiliate's audience arrives with specific context (e.g., "sent by RevOps consultant Jane") and hits a generic homepage that doesn't acknowledge that context. Create dedicated affiliate landing pages that reference the referring source: "Recommended by [Affiliate Name] — here's how their clients use DataFlow." Source-specific landing pages consistently generate 40–60% higher conversion rates than generic homepage drops. Build in PartnerStack's custom landing page feature or use Unbounce with dynamic text replacement.

**Problem: Fraud suspected — unusually high clicks with no conversions**
Solution: Implement a 30-day commission hold (standard practice) to allow refund window and fraud detection. Pause — not cancel — payouts for any affiliate whose click-to-trial ratio exceeds 15:1 in any 7-day window; legitimate newsletter or content traffic converts at 3–7:1. Investigate manually: check referring URLs, IP clustering, and trial email patterns before any account action. If fraud is confirmed, document the pattern, terminate the account, and add detection rule to your fraud filter. Never auto-terminate — false positives damage legitimate affiliate relationships permanently.
