## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Brand account getting flagged or shadowbanned in Reddit subreddits.**
Solution: Audit posting history for vendor-to-value ratio. If more than 1 in 10 posts references your product or company, the account reads as promotional to Reddit's algorithm. Rebuild the account to 95%+ pure educational/peer contribution content for 30 days. For subreddits with strict vendor policies, deploy only the Practitioner Peer persona with zero company affiliation disclosed, and route any direct product questions to a disclosed brand DM rather than public thread.

**Problem: Community monitoring generates too many false positives, overwhelming SDR capacity.**
Solution: Increase signal score threshold for SDR routing from 80 to 90. Add negative keyword filters that exclude signals where the ICP company is outside target firmographic profile. Create tiered queues: automated SDR notification only for 90+, weekly human review for 70–89, AI-only engagement for 50–69, no action for below 50. Revisit ICP company criteria and update firmographic scoring rubric monthly.

**Problem: Community-sourced pipeline is not being tracked — AEs are not recording community source on opportunities.**
Solution: Add community source as a required field in CRM opportunity creation workflow, not optional. Build automatic prompt in deal stage entry form: "Was this contact or company detected in external community monitoring? (Y/N — check #community-intelligence Slack channel for your account)." Create leaderboard of AEs with highest community-attributed closed-won revenue to create competitive tracking incentive.
