## Troubleshooting

**Problem: TikTok Shop affiliate creators receive product but don't post.**
- Root cause: Brief is too restrictive, product doesn't create a visual "wow moment," or creators feel the commission isn't worth their audience relationship.
- Fix: Loosen the brief to "share your honest experience." Add a $50 Amazon gift card bonus for any creator who posts within 14 days. Ensure your product packaging is unboxing-worthy — presentation drives organic content.

**Problem: UGC creative is performing well organically but tanks as paid Spark Ads.**
- Root cause: The organic audience who saw it naturally was already warm/interested. Paid distribution hits cold audiences who need stronger pattern interruption.
- Fix: Select UGC that passes the "5-second cold audience test" — would someone who has never heard of your brand understand the product and feel the problem within 5 seconds? Add text overlays with the product name and key benefit in first 3 seconds.

**Problem: Blended ROAS is below target after 30 days.**
- Root cause: UGC creative pool is too small (algorithm can't optimize), TikTok Shop GMV is being double-counted with paid, or attribution windows are misaligned across platforms.
- Fix: Expand creative pool to minimum 20 assets. Exclude TikTok Shop organic sales from paid ROAS calculation. Standardize on 7-day click / 1-day view attribution across all platforms for apples-to-apples comparison.
