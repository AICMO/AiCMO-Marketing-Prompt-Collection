## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Reels are getting 200-500 views but almost no saves or profile clicks — reach without conversion.**
Solution: This indicates your hooks are working (people are watching) but the content isn't delivering enough value to prompt action. Audit your last 5 Reels — if the value is delivered only verbally and not backed by on-screen text or data, most viewers are consuming passively. Add a specific, take-away framework or stat in on-screen text by second 15. The save trigger for B2B content is "I need to come back to this" — engineer that moment explicitly.

**Problem: Getting followers but they're not the ICP — lifestyle followers, competitor employees, and random accounts.**
Solution: Your hashtag strategy is too broad. Niche down from general #B2B or #SaaS tags to job-title-specific and community-specific tags (e.g., #LegalOps, #ContractManagement, #GC instead of #Business). Also audit your Tier 3 hashtags — if any are >5M posts, they're attracting non-ICP volume. Replace broad tags with 2-3 targeted community-specific hashtags with 20K-200K posts where your actual ICP hangs out. Also: engage proactively by commenting on posts from target accounts before they follow you — this surfaces your account to their audience algorithmically.

**Problem: No DMs from prospects despite growing engagement — people like and save but never reach out.**
Solution: You're missing a friction-reducing bridge between content and conversation. Add a direct DM CTA to 20% of posts: "DM me '[trigger word]' and I'll send you [specific resource]." This makes the ask specific, low-commitment, and immediate. Also ensure your bio CTA is explicit — "DM us 'audit' for a free [X] assessment" consistently outperforms generic "link in bio" bio text for B2B accounts that want to generate prospect conversations rather than just website clicks.
