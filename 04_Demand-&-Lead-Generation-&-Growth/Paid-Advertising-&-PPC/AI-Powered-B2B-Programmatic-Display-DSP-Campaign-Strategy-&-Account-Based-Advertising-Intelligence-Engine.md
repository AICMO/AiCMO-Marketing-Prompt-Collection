## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Low account match rate on named account list (below 50%)**
Solution: Improve input data quality — ensure list includes company domain (not just company name), add known IP ranges if available, supplement with firmographic targeting (SIC codes + company size) as coverage fallback. Switch from IP-based to cookie/device graph-based identity resolution via LiveRamp for better match rates. Expect 50-70% as normal for B2B IP targeting; 70%+ indicates exceptional data quality.

**Problem: High CPM, low impression volume — budget not spending fully**
Solution: Your targeting is likely too narrow. Expand in tiers: (1) widen firmographic parameters by 20% (add adjacent company size bands), (2) add contextual keyword targeting as a reach extension layer alongside IP targeting, (3) reduce minimum viewability threshold from 70% to 60% temporarily to unlock more inventory, (4) move 15-20% of budget from PMPs to open auction if PMP floor prices are too high for your budget.

**Problem: Good CTR but no pipeline influence visible in CRM**
Solution: Programmatic display drives awareness, not direct response — it typically influences pipeline indirectly. Implement view-through conversion tracking (not just click attribution), ensure your impression pixel fires on 70%+ of served impressions (check pixel implementation), create a custom Salesforce report correlating programmatic exposure (account field) with opportunity creation date and stage progression, and run a 90-day incrementality test with a 10% holdout group to prove causal impact vs. correlation.
