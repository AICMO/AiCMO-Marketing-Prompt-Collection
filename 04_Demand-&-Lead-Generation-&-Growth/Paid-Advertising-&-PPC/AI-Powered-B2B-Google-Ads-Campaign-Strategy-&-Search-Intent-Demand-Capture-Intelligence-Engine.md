- Keyword with >200 impressions, CTR <1% → pause or test new ad copy

## Integration Tips

**Google Ads → CRM Pipeline:**
- Connect Google Ads to HubSpot or Salesforce using the native integration or Zapier
- Import offline conversions: Map deal stages (SQL, Opportunity, Closed Won) back to Google Ads click IDs using gclid
- Use conversion values equal to average deal value × stage probability (e.g., SQL = $22K × 20% = $4,400)

**Google Ads → Analytics Stack:**
- Push all campaigns to GA4 using auto-tagging (ensure cross-domain tracking if landing pages are on subdomain)
- Create GA4 audiences from high-intent pageviews (pricing, demo, case studies) and sync to Google Ads for RLSA
- Export Search Terms report weekly to BigQuery via Looker Studio for keyword trend analysis

**Automation Workflows:**
- Zapier: When Google Ads lead form submission → create HubSpot contact → assign to SDR → send lead notification Slack message
- Google Ads Scripts: Automated bid adjustments for day-of-week and hour-of-day performance patterns (free script available in Google Ads library)
- Opteo or PPC Samurai: Automate search term mining and negative keyword recommendations
- Google Ads API: Pull performance data nightly into your marketing data warehouse for unified attribution reporting

**Budget Management:**
- Set up automated budget alerts at 75%, 90%, and 100% of monthly spend via Google Ads notification center
- Use Google Ads shared budgets for campaigns competing for the same audience to prevent over-delivery
- Connect spend data to your marketing dashboard (Looker Studio, Databox) for real-time budget pacing visibility

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: High impressions, low CTR (CTR <1.5% on category campaigns)**
Solution: Your ad copy isn't matching search intent or failing to differentiate from competitors. Run an ad strength audit — ensure at least 5 headlines include specific numbers, outcomes, or the buyer's job title. Review the top 10 search terms driving impressions and verify your headlines use the same language buyers type, not internal product jargon. A/B test one campaign with question-format Headline 1 ("Paying Too Much for AWS?") vs. benefit-led ("Cut AWS Costs by 35%").

**Problem: Strong CTR but low conversion rate (CVR <1.5%)**
Solution: Message mismatch between ad and landing page. Verify the landing page headline mirrors the ad's primary promise word-for-word. Check landing page load time (must be <3 seconds on mobile). Ensure your form is above the fold and asks ≤4 fields (name, email, company, title). Add a trust element near the CTA (customer logo strip, "No credit card required," or a specific social proof stat). Install Microsoft Clarity or Hotjar to watch session recordings and identify drop-off points.

**Problem: Target CPA exceeding goal after 60+ conversions on automated bidding**
Solution: Check for audience overlap cannibalizing your campaigns (brand bidding against generic). Review the conversion lag — if your sales cycle is 30+ days, Google's model needs offline conversion data to optimize toward pipeline, not just form fills. Import CRM data with conversion values and switch from Maximize Conversions to Target ROAS. Alternatively, check for Quality Score degradation (below 5) on high-spend keywords — poor QS inflates CPCs and kills CPA performance; fix with dedicated landing pages that match exact keyword intent.
