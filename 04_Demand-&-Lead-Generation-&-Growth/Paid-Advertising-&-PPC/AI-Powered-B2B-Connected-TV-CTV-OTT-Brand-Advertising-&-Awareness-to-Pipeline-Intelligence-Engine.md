## Overview

This engine designs, launches, and optimizes B2B Connected TV (CTV) and Over-the-Top (OTT) streaming advertising campaigns that build brand awareness among in-market buying committees and convert that awareness into measurable pipeline. Use it when your ICP is reachable on streaming platforms (Hulu, Peacock, Paramount+, YouTube TV, Roku) and you need to run brand-level campaigns that can be tied to downstream revenue — not just impressions.

## Integration Tips

- **The Trade Desk:** Use their "Account-Based Advertising" solution to upload Salesforce account lists and activate CTV targeting natively; connect to your CRM via their Koa AI optimization layer
- **6sense / DemandBase:** Both platforms offer native CTV ad units that bridge your ABM intent data directly into streaming inventory — activate directly from your ABM platform to avoid audience data export/import friction
- **HubSpot / Salesforce:** Create a custom field "CTV Exposed" and use IP-to-account matching (Clearbit, Bombora) to flag accounts that show site visits within 30 days of campaign launch; use this as an enrichment trigger for SDR sequences
- **Google Analytics 4:** Connect YouTube TV to GA4 for cross-channel path analysis; use "Engaged views" as a signal in your attribution model
- **Zapier / Make:** Build a workflow: Account shows 3+ intent signals in Bombora → auto-add to CTV segment in The Trade Desk → trigger SDR task in Salesforce/HubSpot → alert in Slack #hot-accounts channel
- **Metadata.io / Qualified:** Use CTV-exposed account lists as priority queues for AI-powered SDR outreach and website personalization — show different messaging to accounts you know have seen your TV ads

## Troubleshooting

**Issue: Low account match rates (below 30%) when uploading named account list**
Solution: This is common with smaller or international account lists. Use a household graph enrichment service (Experian, Acxiom, LiveRamp) to expand your account data before upload. Supplement with contextual job-title targeting on high-professional-affinity FAST channels (Bloomberg TV, CNBC on Peacock, etc.) where you don't need first-party data to reach executives.

**Issue: CTV campaign is delivering impressions but branded search lift is not materializing after 6 weeks**
Solution: Check frequency — most B2B CTV studies show brand lift doesn't activate until 3+ exposures per household. Pull frequency report; if average frequency is below 2.5, either increase budget or tighten audience (reduce scale to improve frequency for core ICP). Also verify your branded search baseline — if there was a PR event or product launch during the campaign, it may be inflating organic search and masking lift.

**Issue: CFO/CEO is skeptical of CTV because "we can't prove direct attribution"**
Solution: Set up a geo-holdout test before launch: identify 2 comparable metros to your test markets and withhold CTV spend there. After 60–90 days, compare: (1) pipeline generated in test vs. control geos, (2) branded search volume, (3) time-to-close for accounts in each geo. This creates a defensible incrementality case. Also pull account-level data from 6sense/DemandBase showing that CTV-exposed accounts have higher intent scores and shorter sales cycles than unexposed accounts.
