Phase 2: Optimization (Days 15-30)
- Winning creative criteria: Hook Rate > 30% AND VTR 75% > 15% AND CTR > 1%

- Audience expansion: Enable "Reach & Frequency" for top-performing creatives once CPL is stable

Phase 3: Scaling (Days 31+)
- Horizontal scaling: Duplicate winning ad group into new audience segments
- Vertical scaling: Increase budget 20% every 48-72 hours

- Lookalike expansion: Test 2% and 3% lookalike once 1% is profitable

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Ad account in "learning limited" status after 2+ weeks**
Solution: Your conversion event is too rare for the algorithm to learn from. Switch from "Lead" optimization to "Click" optimization in the learning phase. Aim for 50+ clicks per day minimum before switching back to Lead objective. Alternatively, broaden your targeting (remove restrictive layers) temporarily to generate more signal.

**Problem: High form views but low submission rate (<8%)**
Solution: Your form has too much friction or the value exchange feels unequal. Reduce custom questions to 1 (remove all non-essential fields — every additional field drops completion by 10-15%). Rewrite the form headline to emphasize what they get (e.g., "Get your personalized ROI analysis" not "Book a demo"). Test an A/B version with only Name + Work Email.

**Problem: Strong creative metrics (high VTR, CTR) but terrible CPL**
Solution: Traffic is engaged but converting poorly. The creative is attracting the wrong audience (general interest vs. actual buyers). Add a self-qualifying statement in the hook: "If you're managing a team of 10+ salespeople..." Review your landing page offer — if you're sending TikTok traffic to a generic homepage, switch to a dedicated landing page with the same message as the ad (message match). Also check that mobile landing page loads in under 3 seconds.
