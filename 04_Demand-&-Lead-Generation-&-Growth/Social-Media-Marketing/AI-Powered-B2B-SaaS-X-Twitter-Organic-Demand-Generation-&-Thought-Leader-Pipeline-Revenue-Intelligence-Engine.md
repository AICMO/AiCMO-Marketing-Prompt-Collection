## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Content gets impressions but no inbound DMs or link clicks**
Fix: Your content is educating the wrong audience (non-buyers who enjoy learning but won't buy). Audit your last 30 top-performing posts: what titles/companies do the engagers hold? If they don't match your ICP, your topics are too generic. Shift from broad "SaaS growth" themes to hyper-specific ICP pain points (e.g., not "how to improve sales forecasting" but "why CROs at $50M ARR companies miss their Q4 number"). Add a direct CTA in the final tweet of every thread — most marketers skip this and lose 70% of conversion opportunity.

**Problem: Follower growth is stagnant after first 30 days**
Fix: You're in "posting mode" but not "reply mode." The X algorithm rewards accounts that generate conversation, not just content. Spend 50% of your daily X time replying to anchor accounts and ICP executive tweets — not generic "great post!" but substantive 2-3 sentence responses that add a data point, challenge an assumption, or share a customer anecdote. This surfaces you in the feeds of their audiences. Target: 10-15 high-quality replies per day minimum.

**Problem: Cannot attribute X leads accurately — "direct traffic" catch-all absorbs X clicks**
Fix: Three-layer attribution system: (1) UTM-tag every link across every X surface (bio, pinned tweet, individual tweet links) with unique UTM combinations per asset. (2) Add self-reported attribution field to every form with "X / Twitter" as an explicit option — buyers who found you through X overwhelmingly select it when given the choice. (3) Set up a dedicated landing page URL that is ONLY shared on X (e.g., yourdomain.com/x) — any traffic to this URL is definitionally X-sourced, UTMs or not. The intersection of these three signals captures 85%+ of X-generated demand.
