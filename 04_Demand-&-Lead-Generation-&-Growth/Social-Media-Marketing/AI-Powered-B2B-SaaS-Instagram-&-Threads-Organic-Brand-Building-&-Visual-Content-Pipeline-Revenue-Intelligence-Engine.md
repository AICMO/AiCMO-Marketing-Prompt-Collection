## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Instagram content gets good reach but zero link clicks or demo requests**
Solution: The disconnect is usually between top-of-funnel content (education, entertainment) and conversion architecture. Audit the call-to-action in every post — "link in bio" is too weak; replace with specific CTAs: "Save the [Resource Name] — link in bio → [Resource]." Second, ensure the Linktree/bio link destination matches the content promise exactly. If a post is about a customer achieving X result, the bio link should go directly to that case study, not the homepage. Third, add mid-funnel content: posts that assume the viewer already knows the problem exists and are ready to evaluate solutions — include explicit CTAs like "If your team is dealing with [specific scenario], we built something for this."

**Problem: Threads posts get views but no replies or follower growth**
Solution: Threads' algorithm rewards conversation starters over broadcasts. Shift from "here's what we know" format to "here's a question I genuinely don't know the answer to" or "here's a take I know is going to be controversial." End every Threads post with a direct question — not "what do you think?" (too vague) but "CFOs in Series B: is [specific assumption] actually true for your team, or am I wrong?" Specificity drives replies. Also: reply to every comment within 2 hours of posting — Threads heavily weights conversation depth in its early algorithm.

**Problem: Can't produce Reels consistently — team doesn't have video skills**
Solution: Eliminate video as the default format and instead use Instagram's "Slides" Reel format (a Reel made entirely of static images with text overlays that auto-advances like a slideshow). This format performs comparably to polished video for B2B audiences, requires only Canva, and can be produced in 30 minutes. Build 5 Canva templates with pre-set transitions and text placement — every Reel is then a matter of populating the template with new data or customer quotes. Once this cadence is established and reach grows, invest in one 90-minute "batch recording" session per month where an executive records 4 talking-head Reels in a single sitting.
