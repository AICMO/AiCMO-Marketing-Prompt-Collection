## Integration Tips

**Shopify:** Connect Pinterest Sales Channel directly — syncs product catalog automatically, installs base tag, and enables checkout on Pinterest (native checkout reduces friction). Enable "catalog sync" for real-time inventory and pricing updates.

**Google Merchant Center:** If running Google Shopping, your existing product feed can be adapted for Pinterest with minimal edits (add `g:availability` and ensure image dimensions are 2:3). Use a feed management tool like DataFeedWatch or GoDataFeed for multi-channel feed management.

**Klaviyo/Email Platform:** Sync your abandoned cart and purchaser email segments to Pinterest Audiences monthly. These become your highest-performing retargeting pools and source audiences for Actalikes. Create a Zapier automation: new Klaviyo segment → trigger Pinterest audience update.

**Triple Whale / Northbeam / Rockerbox:** Connect Pinterest as a paid channel to get de-duplicated attribution across Meta, Google, and Pinterest. Pinterest often gets credit for assists rather than last-click conversions — MMM tools will show its true contribution.

**Notion/Airtable Creative Tracker:** Log all creative assets with format, launch date, performance metrics, and retirement date. This prevents running expired seasonal creative and maintains a feedback loop between marketing and design on what visual styles are converting.

**Google Looker Studio:** Build a Pinterest performance dashboard pulling from the Pinterest API. Key dimensions: campaign, ad group, ad, device, gender, age. Key metrics: impressions, saves, clicks, CTR, CPC, conversions, ROAS, spend.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: High impressions but very low CTR (<0.1%)**
*Cause:* Creative not native to Pinterest's visual aesthetic — likely looks like an ad rather than an organic pin. Pinterest users scroll past anything that feels promotional.
*Fix:* Replace product-on-white-background images with lifestyle scenes. Remove "Shop Now" text overlays embedded in images. Lead with the aspirational outcome (beautiful bedroom, perfect dinner table) not the product. Ensure images are 2:3 vertical — landscape images are demoted by the algorithm.

**Problem: Shopping campaigns showing catalog rejections (15%+ rejection rate)**
*Cause:* Common rejection reasons: images under 600x900px, price mismatch between feed and landing page, restricted categories (alcohol, adult), watermarks on images, non-working landing page URLs.
*Fix:* Download rejection report from Pinterest Ads Manager → Catalogs → Feed Health. Fix most common rejection reason first. For price mismatches, ensure Shopify prices sync within 24 hours of any sale. Enable automatic feed refresh (every 24 hours minimum).

**Problem: Retargeting ROAS strong but prospecting ROAS well below target after 60 days**
*Cause:* Pinterest prospecting requires longer learning periods than Meta/Google because purchase cycles are longer (Pinners save and return weeks later). Also, interest targeting alone without keyword refinement reaches too broad an audience.
*Fix:* Add keyword targeting alongside interest targeting (AND logic, not OR). Narrow audience to top-performing age/gender segments. Shift budget toward 1% Actalike (most similar to purchasers) and reduce broad interest allocation. Extend attribution window to 30-day click to capture delayed conversions.
