## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Performance Max is spending all budget but generating zero conversions**
Solution: PMax needs strong audience signals to find converters. Upload a customer list of minimum 1,000 emails, add custom intent audiences based on your top converting search queries, and set Final URL Expansion to OFF to control landing page quality. Also verify your conversion tracking is firing correctly — PMax will spend on the path of least resistance if it cannot find conversion data. Give the campaign 2-3 weeks with no changes after providing audience signals.

**Problem: Smart bidding CPA is 2-3x higher than target after 30 days**
Solution: Smart bidding requires 30-50 conversions per campaign per month to exit learning mode. If volume is too low, either (1) switch to Maximize Conversions with no CPA target for 30 days to build data, (2) add micro-conversions as secondary actions to increase the conversion signal volume, or (3) consolidate campaigns to pool conversion data. Never change bids or budgets more than 20% in any 7-day period — this resets the learning phase.

**Problem: Competitor keywords have high CPC but low conversion rates**
Solution: Competitor campaigns require dedicated landing pages that specifically address the comparison: "How Meridian compares to Looker" with a feature comparison table, migration guide offer, and customer quote from a switcher. Generic homepage traffic from competitor keywords converts at 40-60% lower rates. Also consider using Dynamic Search Ads (DSA) with your competitor comparison pages as the URL target, which Google often rewards with better Quality Scores than pure keyword bidding for competitive terms.
