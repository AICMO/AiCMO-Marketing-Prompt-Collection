## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Creator content drives views but zero qualified traffic**
Root cause: Content brief is too product-focused, not audience-problem-focused. Creators' audiences skip sponsor sections when they feel like ads.
Fix: Rebuild brief around a specific pain point the ICP urgently cares about. Give the creator latitude to integrate your tool into their own narrative. Test "tutorial format" (creator shows how they use the product) vs. "mention format." Also verify UTM links are working — broken links are often the real issue.

**Problem: Strong CTR but poor trial/demo conversion rate (landing page disconnect)**
Root cause: Creator's description of your product creates expectations your landing page doesn't meet, or the landing page is generic while the audience came with specific context.
Fix: Build creator-specific landing pages that mirror the creator's language and the specific use case covered in the video. "Welcome [Creator Name] audience — here's the [workflow/feature] they showed you." Conversion rates typically improve 40-80% with context-specific landing pages.

**Problem: Attribution shows negligible revenue, but self-reported survey says YouTube is top source**
Root cause: UTM attribution model (last-click) doesn't capture long B2B sales cycles. YouTube creates awareness that converts to organic search 30-90 days later — and last-click credits "google / organic" instead.
Fix: Implement data-driven attribution in GA4, extend lookback window to 90 days, and weight self-reported survey data alongside UTM data. Consider marketing mix modeling (MMM) if budget allows. Add "How did you hear about us?" to your demo request form and track YouTube + specific creator name mentions.
