## Integration Tips

- **HubSpot:** Use HubSpot's Partner Relationship Management (PRM) module or a custom "Partner" company property. Automate deal registration confirmations with workflows. Sync partner pipeline to a dedicated HubSpot pipeline stage for partner-sourced attribution.
- **Salesforce:** Enable Partner Community licenses ($15/user/month) for deal registration portal. Use Salesforce PRM or a lightweight tool like Alliances by Impartner synced to Salesforce. Create a "Partner Source" field on Opportunity to track attribution.
- **Google Sheets + Zapier:** For early-stage programs, track partner health scores in a Google Sheet. Use Zapier to auto-update activity scores from HubSpot/CRM data and trigger Slack alerts when a partner goes inactive.
- **n8n / Make (Integromat):** Automate the AI asset generation workflow: partner submits asset request form → n8n triggers Claude/GPT-4 API call with partner profile → generates co-branded copy → emails draft to partner in <2 hours.
- **Notion:** Build your partner portal as a Notion workspace with shared pages per partner tier. Use Notion AI to draft deal support content on demand. Gate Tier 1 content behind partner authentication.
- **Slack:** Create a dedicated #partner-wins channel. Auto-post deal registrations and closed-won partner deals using Zapier → Slack. Public celebration of partner wins builds program culture.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Partners sign up but go inactive after 30 days, never using assets or registering deals.**
Solution: This is almost always an activation failure, not a partner quality problem. Add a mandatory "first campaign launch" milestone at Day 21 of onboarding — require partners to send the Book of Business Blast campaign before they can access MDF funds. Pair with a 30-minute co-sell kickoff call where you help them identify the first 10 target accounts together. Remove friction from the asset request process: if partners have to ask a human for materials, they won't. Deploy a self-serve asset generator in the partner portal powered by a simple intake form + AI API call.

**Problem: Channel conflict — direct sales team is closing deals that partners sourced, creating friction and damaging partner trust.**
Solution: Implement strict deal registration with a 90-day protection window and enforce a spiff/margin differential (minimum 5% additional margin) for registered deals. Create a documented Rules of Engagement policy signed by the CRO and VP of Partnerships. Add a "Partner Source" field to all CRM opportunities and make it a required field before AE compensation is calculated. Run a monthly channel conflict review between sales leadership and partner team to catch violations early.

**Problem: Partners claim they're generating referrals but pipeline quality is poor — low conversion, wrong ICP, small deals.**
Solution: Tighten the deal registration form to require: company name, contact name/title, deal size estimate, use case, and why the partner believes this is qualified. Add a 48-hour partner qualification call standard before the deal is officially registered. Run quarterly "partner ICP calibration" sessions where you show partners 5 examples of your best-fit customers (with permission) and 5 examples of poor-fit accounts, and explain why. Adjust MDF eligibility to require a minimum deal size and ICP match score — this quickly self-selects for partners who understand your buyer.
