## Success Metrics

- **Partner recruitment rate:** % of outreach that converts to signed partner agreements (benchmark: 8–15% for cold outreach to warm agency contacts)
- **Partner activation rate:** % of signed partners who submit their first referral within 90 days (benchmark: 40–60% for programs with structured onboarding)
- **Partner-sourced pipeline as % of total pipeline:** Target 10–25% within 12 months depending on program maturity
- **Partner-sourced win rate vs. direct:** Partner-sourced deals should close at 15–30% higher win rate than direct (social proof effect from agency recommendation)
- **Partner revenue per active partner (RPAP):** Total partner-sourced ARR / active partners (benchmark: $30K–$80K RPAP for RevOps-category programs)
- **Partner retention rate:** % of partners still active at 12 months (target: 70%+ for healthy programs)
- **Time to first referral per new partner:** Under 45 days indicates strong onboarding; over 90 days signals activation failure

## Integration Tips

**HubSpot:**
- Create a custom "Partner Source" deal property with dropdown: Referral / Resell / Co-Sell / Influenced
- Use HubSpot's Partner Program module (or PRM integrations like PartnerStack, Impartner) to track deal registrations
- Build a Partner Dashboard report showing MRR sourced by agency, win rate, and ACV per partner

**Salesforce:**
- Use Partner Community (Experience Cloud) as the partner portal for deal registration, content access, and training
- Add "Partner Account" object linked to Opportunity for revenue attribution
- Configure Salesforce PRM's partner scorecard to auto-calculate tier status quarterly

**PartnerStack / Impartner / Crossbeam:**
- PartnerStack: Best for referral/affiliate-style agency programs with clean commission tracking
- Crossbeam: Use for partner account mapping to identify overlapping customers before co-marketing
- Impartner: Best for mature enterprise partner programs needing full PRM workflow automation

**Notion/Confluence:**
- Build a Partner Playbook wiki as the single source of truth for all agency-facing content, updated quarterly
- Use Notion databases for partner tier tracking before you invest in a full PRM

**Zapier/Make:**
- Automate: Deal registration → CRM opportunity creation → Partner notification email → Commission calculation
- Trigger: Partner certification complete → Tier upgrade workflow → MDF allocation notification

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Agencies sign up but never submit a referral (activation failure)**
Solution: The top cause is agencies not having a natural moment to recommend your product. Fix: Provide a "Stack Assessment" template agencies can use with all clients that includes your product category as a natural evaluation point. Make the ask specific: "Who among your current clients are struggling with pipeline accuracy?" rather than "Let us know when an opportunity comes up."

**Problem: Partner-sourced pipeline has lower quality than direct pipeline (low ICP fit)**
Solution: Your agency partners may be referring everyone they know rather than qualifying first. Fix: Add an ICP alignment session to Week 2 of onboarding (not Week 8). Give agencies a 5-question client qualification checklist. Consider gating deal registration so partners must confirm ICP fit before a deal is registered—this reduces junk deals and trains partners faster.

**Problem: Top-tier agencies demand exclusivity before joining**
Solution: Rarely grant exclusivity—it destroys program scale. Instead, offer "preferred partner" status with enhanced MDF, co-branding rights, and joint case study promotion as a near-exclusivity alternative. Frame it as: "We work with multiple agency partners because our clients choose their agencies—but Preferred Partners get first access to joint opportunities and marketing investment."
