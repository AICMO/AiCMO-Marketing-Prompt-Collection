## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Partners sign program agreements but never activate (go dark after onboarding week 1)**

*Solution:* The most common cause is that the value exchange isn't immediate enough. Fix with three changes: (1) Create a "Quick Win" within the first 7 days — a co-branded one-pager they can send to their sales team immediately, (2) Schedule a mandatory "account mapping" call in week 2 where you identify 5 shared prospects to pursue together, (3) Assign a named partner success owner (even if they handle 20 partners) who sends a personal check-in on day 8. Partners go inactive when they feel like a low priority.

**Problem: App marketplace listing getting installs but few convert to paid customers (high churn, low LTV from marketplace channel)**

*Solution:* Marketplace installs attract users seeking free/exploratory access. Fix by: (1) Tightening your listing to attract ICP-fit buyers (add company size/role language to your description), (2) Implementing a marketplace-specific onboarding sequence that connects marketplace users to a sales rep after day 3 of activity, (3) Adding a "For Enterprise Teams" CTA to the listing that routes high-value visitors to a direct sales contact form rather than the self-serve flow.

**Problem: Partners unwilling to share their customer lists for co-marketing because of data privacy concerns**

*Solution:* Never ask for raw customer lists — this is a compliance and trust issue. Instead: (1) Use Crossbeam or Reveal for privacy-safe account overlap (neither party sees the other's full data, only matched accounts), (2) Propose partner-to-partner email campaigns where the partner sends the email to their own list on your behalf (you provide the creative, they maintain data control), (3) Design co-marketing around events and webinars where customers self-register rather than being targeted directly.
