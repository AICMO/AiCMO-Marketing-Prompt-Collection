## Overview
Designs and operationalizes a full hyperscaler alliance marketing program — AWS, Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud — that converts cloud partnerships from passive logos on your website into active, co-funded, co-sold revenue engines generating partner-sourced pipeline within 90 days. Use this when you have achieved a Tier 1 or Advanced partner designation with a major cloud provider but are generating fewer than 10% of pipeline through the co-sell motion, when your cloud marketplace listing exists but drives minimal inbound, or when you want to leverage hyperscaler MDF (Market Development Funds) to amplify demand generation at zero or reduced cost.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: PDMs won't accept co-sell registrations or go silent after initial intro**
Solution: Your pitch is likely framing value in terms of your product features rather than the cloud provider's goals. Rewrite your PDM pitch to lead with: "here's how our joint customers increase [AWS/Azure/GCP] spend" and "here's the customer problem your field team has already heard about that we solve." PDMs co-sell when it makes them look good to their customers AND increases cloud consumption.

**Problem: Marketplace listing getting traffic but no conversions**
Solution: Run a private offer experiment — reach out to 10 current deal prospects who have cloud spending commitments and offer to structure the purchase as a marketplace private offer. The issue is often discovery (listing optimization needed) or friction in trial-to-paid (add a "Contact Sales" CTA to your listing, not just a 14-day trial). Also check your listing against competitors in the marketplace to audit any differentiation gaps.

**Problem: MDF budget going unspent because campaigns aren't getting approved**
Solution: Submit MDF proposals at least 45 days before the campaign start date (not after). Ensure all activities have a co-branding requirement and lead capture mechanism. If proposals keep getting rejected, ask your PDM directly what categories of spend are easiest to get approved in the current quarter and design activities around those constraints first to build trust and a track record before proposing larger or more creative initiatives.
