## Success Metrics

- **Retargeting ROAS:** B2B target 4:1+ on pipeline influenced; D2C target 6:1+ on direct revenue
- **BOFU CVR lift:** Retargeted BOFU visitors should convert at 3-5x the site baseline (if baseline is 0.7%, target 2.5-4% from retargeted BOFU segment)
- **Frequency efficiency:** CTR should stay above 0.8% on Meta and 0.4% on LinkedIn; if dropping, creative fatigue has set in — swap before frequency cap is hit
- **Segment overlap rate:** Use platform overlap tools to confirm segments are not cannibalizing each other — target under 15% overlap between adjacent segments
- **Cost per incremental conversion:** Use geo holdout or conversion lift test after 60 days to measure true incrementality — retargeting can claim credit for conversions that would have happened organically
- **Audience growth rate:** BOFU segment should grow proportionally with traffic — if segment size is flat while traffic is growing, check pixel firing rules

## Integration Tips
- **Google Tag Manager:** Set up custom triggers for high-intent behaviors (pricing page scroll depth 75%, demo button click, ROI calculator interaction) to build more precise BOFU audiences than simple URL-based retargeting. Export a GTM container snippet with all triggers pre-configured as part of this prompt's output.
- **HubSpot / Salesforce:** Create CRM-synced audiences by exporting lifecycle stage lists (MQL, SQL, Opportunity, Closed Lost) as CSV for Meta Custom Audiences and LinkedIn Matched Audiences. Automate the export weekly via HubSpot's LinkedIn integration or Salesforce's Meta Business extension — stale CRM lists are one of the top causes of retargeting waste.
- **Segment (CDP):** If running Segment, use Personas to create computed traits ("visited pricing 2+ times in 30 days," "used ROI calculator") and sync directly to ad platform destinations — no manual CSV uploads, real-time audience updates.
- **Google Analytics 4:** Build GA4 audiences using event-based conditions (page_view where page_path contains /pricing AND session_duration greater than 60 seconds) and publish directly to Google Ads for Display retargeting. This filters out low-quality bounces before they enter your paid retargeting pool.
- **Zapier / Make:** Build an automation: when a HubSpot contact's lifecycle stage changes to "Customer," trigger an API call to Meta's Conversions API to add them to the suppression (exclusion) audience — prevents serving ads to paying customers, which damages brand perception and wastes budget.
- **Northbeam / Triple Whale (D2C):** For e-commerce, connect your MTA (multi-touch attribution) platform to see retargeting's true contribution vs. claimed last-click credit. This data should drive budget reallocation decisions in Week 4 of the optimization checklist.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: BOFU retargeting audiences are too small to activate (under 100 users on Meta, under 300 on LinkedIn)**
Solution: Broaden the BOFU definition to include all visitors who spent 90+ seconds on site AND visited any product-related page (not just pricing). Alternatively, extend the recency window from 14 days to 30 days. On LinkedIn specifically, combine website retargeting with a CRM matched audience upload to hit the 300-member minimum. If traffic volume is the root cause, prioritize growing organic and paid search traffic before heavy retargeting investment — retargeting amplifies existing funnel volume; it does not replace it.

**Problem: High CTR on retargeting ads but no conversion lift — people click but still do not convert**
Solution: The problem is the landing page experience, not the ad creative. Retargeted visitors who click have demonstrated intent — if they are not converting, the page is not matching their expectation or funnel stage. Audit three things: (1) Does the page have segment-specific messaging, or is it generic? (2) Is the conversion ask appropriately sized for their stage — a demo request shown to a TOFU visitor is too aggressive? (3) Is social proof visible above the fold? Run a 5-second test — if users cannot identify the value proposition and next step in 5 seconds, rebuild the above-fold section before touching ad creative.

**Problem: Retargeting ads burning through budget with no frequency control — same user seeing ads 20+ times per week**
Solution: Frequency caps are not set or are too loose. On Meta: set campaign-level frequency cap in Reach & Frequency buying or use ad set budget limits as a proxy control. On LinkedIn: use the Frequency column in Campaign Manager — pause campaigns where frequency exceeds 5/week for BOFU and 2/week for TOFU. On Google Display: set frequency cap at 3 impressions/day, 15/week per campaign. Also audit audience size — if your retargeting pool is under 500 users but you are spending $2,000/month against it, budget concentration is the root cause, not the cap settings themselves.
