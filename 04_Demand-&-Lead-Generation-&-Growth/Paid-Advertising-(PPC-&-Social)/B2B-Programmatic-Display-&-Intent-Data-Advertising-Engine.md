## Integration Tips

**6sense:**
- Use 6sense Audience Builder to create Tier A/B/C segments based on AI-generated account score + intent surge data
- Connect 6sense to Salesforce via native integration: map "6sense Intent" and "6sense Stage" to Opportunity and Lead fields
- Enable 6sense Web Personalization to serve dynamic content to visiting accounts from your Tier B/C lists simultaneously with display ads (multiplier effect)
- Set up 6sense Activity Alerts to notify SDRs when a Tier 1 account hits "Decision" stage — programmatic warms the account, SDR closes the loop

**DemandBase:**
- Use DemandBase One's Journey Stage to auto-move accounts between campaign tiers without manual updates
- Pull DemandBase "Pipeline Predict" score for Tier C acceleration budget decisions
- Integrate DemandBase with HubSpot via the native connector; map DB account engagement score to HubSpot Company Score field

**Salesforce + HubSpot:**
- Create a custom Campaign Influence model that gives credit to programmatic impressions using Salesforce Campaign Influence (first-touch, last-touch, and linear models)
- Build a "Programmatic Influenced Pipeline" report: Opportunities where the Account was in a programmatic campaign audience AND received >10 impressions in the 90 days prior to opportunity creation date
- Tag all programmatic campaigns with UTM parameters: utm_source=programmatic, utm_medium=display, utm_campaign=[tier]-[stage]-[persona], utm_content=[creative-variant]

**Zapier / Make (Automation):**
- Trigger: Account moves to Tier B in 6sense → Zap → Add to Salesforce Campaign → Notify SDR owner via Slack
- Trigger: Opportunity created for account in Tier C programmatic audience → Zap → Increase Tier C bid cap by 20% → Log touch in Opportunity activity

**Notion / Google Sheets:**
- Maintain a weekly "Account Signal Board" in Notion: columns = Account Name, Tier, Intent Score (week-over-week), Impressions Delivered, Site Visits, Open Opp (Y/N), Owner
- Use Google Sheets + Google Analytics API to pull website visit data from targeted account IP ranges weekly for accounts not yet CRM-connected

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Match rate is below 50% — you're serving impressions but can't confirm account identity**
Platform reports show impressions delivered, but your 6sense or DemandBase dashboard shows <50% of target accounts actually reached.
→ Fix: Your account list likely has stale domains or subsidiary entities not matching the DSP's identity graph. Re-upload the account list with both parent domain AND subsidiary domains. Use LinkedIn Matched Audiences (company list upload) as a parallel channel — LinkedIn's match rate for named B2B accounts is typically 60-80% vs. 40-60% for open-web DSPs. Also: ensure your account list uses the same domain format the DSP expects (no "www.", standardize to root domain).

**Problem: High reach, high frequency, zero pipeline influence — ads are running but nothing is happening**
You're hitting frequency targets but the influenced pipeline report shows <1% of reached accounts have opened opportunities.
→ Fix: First, verify the CRM integration is actually logging programmatic touches (check a sample of 10 Tier B accounts — do they have programmatic campaign membership in Salesforce?). If integration is broken, fix it before blaming creative. If integration is working, the issue is usually creative-audience mismatch: your creative is talking about features your audience doesn't yet care about for their stage. Run a creative audit — pull CTR by creative variant and replace anything below 0.05% CTR. Also check if your landing pages match the ad message (mismatched landing pages kill post-click conversion regardless of ad quality).

**Problem: Sales team isn't acting on programmatic-influenced accounts**
Marketing reports show 25 programmatic-influenced accounts, but SDRs say they don't know which accounts to call.
→ Fix: The programmatic data is in Salesforce but salespeople don't look at Campaign Influence reports. Build a "Hot Account" Salesforce List View that filters for: Account is in programmatic Tier B or C campaign AND Intent Score increased in last 7 days AND No activity from SDR in last 14 days. Set a Salesforce Flow to send a daily Slack digest to each SDR with their top 5 warming accounts. The rule: if marketing is warming the account in display, sales must touch within 5 business days or the account moves to competitor territory.
