## Success Metrics

- **Cost Per Result (CPR):** Benchmark against your industry — D2C purchase CPA typically $25-$80; B2B lead CPA typically $50-$200
- **Return on Ad Spend (ROAS):** Target 2.5x+ for e-commerce at steady state; measure on 7-day click / 1-day view window
- **Click-Through Rate (CTR):** Link CTR >1.5% for feed placements; >0.8% for Reels/Stories indicates healthy creative
- **Frequency:** Keep TOFU frequency <2.5 per 7 days before refreshing creative; MOFU can run to 5-7 before fatigue
- **Cost Per Thousand Impressions (CPM):** Benchmark $10-$30 for US consumer; $30-$80 for US B2B professional audiences
- **Event Match Quality (EMQ):** 7.0+ score confirms strong pixel signal and proper CAPI implementation
- **Landing Page Conversion Rate:** Meta traffic typically converts at 1-3%; gap below 1% signals landing page, not ad, problem

## Integration Tips

- **Meta Ads Manager + Google Sheets:** Use the Meta Ads reporting API or Supermetrics to pull daily campaign data into a Google Sheet dashboard; automate weekly performance summaries with a Zapier + ChatGPT workflow
- **HubSpot / Salesforce:** Append UTM parameters to all ad URLs (`utm_source=facebook&utm_medium=cpc&utm_campaign={{campaign.name}}&utm_content={{ad.name}}`); create a CRM view filtering contacts by `Original Source = Paid Social` to measure pipeline influenced
- **Klaviyo / Mailchimp:** Sync your email list as a Custom Audience in Meta; suppress active customers from prospecting campaigns and create a 1% lookalike from your highest-LTV email segment
- **Shopify:** Enable Meta's native Shopify integration for server-side event matching; use the Shopify pixel helper to verify purchase event deduplication
- **Slack Alerts via Zapier:** Trigger a Slack message when daily spend exceeds budget cap by 15%, or when CPM spikes >40% above the 7-day average — catches budget misallocations before they burn budget
- **Triple Whale / Northbeam:** Layer a third-party attribution tool for D2C brands to get platform-agnostic ROAS that reconciles Meta's self-reported numbers against actual revenue

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Campaign stuck in learning phase for more than 7 days (Learning Limited status)**
Solution: Consolidate ad sets — Meta requires ~50 optimization events per ad set per week to exit learning. Merge low-volume ad sets targeting similar audiences into one. Switch from a narrow conversion event (Purchase) to a higher-volume event (Add to Cart or Initiate Checkout) temporarily to accumulate signal faster, then graduate back to Purchase once volume is sufficient.

**Problem: Strong CTR but low conversion rate (traffic arriving but not converting)**
Solution: Diagnose at the landing page, not the ad. Check that headline message exactly mirrors the ad promise — a disconnect between "50% off today" in the ad and no visible offer on the landing page causes immediate drop-off. Verify mobile page load speed is under 3 seconds (use Google PageSpeed Insights). Ensure the primary CTA is above the fold on mobile. Run a 5-second test: can a stranger read the headline and understand the offer immediately?

**Problem: Rapidly rising CPMs with no change in budget or targeting**
Solution: Audience fatigue is compressing your reach. Check frequency — if TOFU frequency exceeds 3.0 in a 7-day window, your audience has seen your ads too many times. Introduce 2-3 new creative variants immediately. Expand the audience size (loosen demographic filters or switch to Advantage+ Audience). Test a new interest cluster to find untapped audiences. Alternatively, pause for 3-5 days and re-enter the auction fresh.
