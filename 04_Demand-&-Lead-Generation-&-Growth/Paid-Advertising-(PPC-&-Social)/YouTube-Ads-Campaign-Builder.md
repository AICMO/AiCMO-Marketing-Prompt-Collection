## Integration Tips

- **Google Ads:** Import the campaign structure directly — use the Google Ads Editor CSV template to bulk-upload ad groups, audiences, and bid modifiers generated from this prompt.
- **GA4:** Use the measurement framework section to configure custom events (video_25, video_50, video_75, video_complete) as micro-conversions; import these into Google Ads for smarter bidding signals.
- **HubSpot/Salesforce:** Tag inbound demo requests with UTM parameters (`utm_source=youtube&utm_campaign=ToF|MoF|BoF`) to pipe funnel-stage data into CRM and calculate true pipeline CPA by campaign tier.
- **Zapier:** Connect Google Ads lead form extensions directly to HubSpot via Zapier — new YouTube lead form submission triggers contact creation + deal stage "Demo Requested" automatically.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: View rate below 15% on skippable ads**
Fix: The first 5 seconds are not compelling enough. Rewrite the hook — lead with a provocative question, a counterintuitive statement, or a bold visual that creates a "wait, what?" moment. Avoid logo-first openers; lead with conflict or curiosity.

**Problem: High view rate but zero site visits or conversions**
Fix: The CTA is unclear or the landing page does not match the ad's promise. Ensure the overlay CTA button text matches exactly what the viewer will get (e.g., "Get Free Demo" not "Learn More"). Check landing page load speed on mobile — 80%+ of YouTube traffic is mobile.

**Problem: Target CPA is being missed by 50%+ after week 3**
Fix: The BoF remarketing audience is too small (under 1,000 users). Expand ToF spend by 30% to fill the funnel, lower the learning phase CPA target to 2x your actual target so Google's algorithm does not over-constrain delivery, and add Customer Match lists to anchor the audience signal.
