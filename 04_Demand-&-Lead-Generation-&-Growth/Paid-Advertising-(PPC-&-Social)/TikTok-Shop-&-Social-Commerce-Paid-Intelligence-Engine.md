## Integration Tips

- **TikTok Ads Manager → Shopify:** Enable TikTok Sales Channel app to sync product catalog, enable pixel events (ViewContent, AddToCart, Purchase), and automate Shop Ad creation from Shopify collections
- **TikTok Creator Marketplace → Grin/Aspire:** Export creator campaign performance data to your influencer platform for unified ROI reporting across TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube creator programs
- **TikTok Pixel → Triple Whale / Northbeam:** Feed TikTok click and view-through data into your MMM/attribution tool to get blended ROAS and incrementality measurements, especially important given iOS attribution gaps
- **TikTok Shop → Klaviyo:** Sync TikTok Shop customer purchase data to Klaviyo for post-purchase sequences, win-back campaigns, and LTV-based segmentation (separate flow for TikTok-acquired customers)
- **Spark Ads → Notion/Airtable:** Build a creator content library tracking which organic posts you've whitelisted, performance benchmarks, and creator contract status for scalable program management

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: High CTR but low conversion rate from TikTok Shop ads**
Solution: The disconnect is usually between ad promise and landing experience. Audit your TikTok Shop product listing against what the ad shows — ensure the thumbnail, price, and product description immediately match the ad's hook. Also check your shop's review count; fewer than 50 reviews will tank conversion. Consider driving to your own landing page with stronger social proof before the Shop tab.

**Problem: Spark Ads performing worse than regular in-feed ads**
Solution: You're likely boosting posts without sufficient organic engagement signals. Only amplify posts that have already reached ≥3% organic engagement rate and show positive sentiment in comments. Also ensure the creator has authorized the Spark Ad correctly (check authorization code expiration — they expire in 30/60/90 days). Poorly authorized Spark Ads lose the native "social proof" halo effect.

**Problem: Creator affiliate program not driving attributable revenue**
Solution: TikTok's default attribution window for affiliates is 7-day last-click, which misses view-through conversions. Switch to 30-day attribution in TikTok Shop's affiliate settings. Also, many creators won't add Shop links consistently — create a weekly check-in routine or automate link verification via the Creator Marketplace dashboard. Provide creators with a "link reminder" template to use in LIVE sessions and video descriptions.
