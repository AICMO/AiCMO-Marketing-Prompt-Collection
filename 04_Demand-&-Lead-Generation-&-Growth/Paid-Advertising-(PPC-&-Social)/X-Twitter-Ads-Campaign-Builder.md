## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Ads stuck in review or repeatedly rejected**
Solution: X Ads policies prohibit certain financial, health, and political claims. Review X's Ads Policy for your vertical. Avoid superlatives ("best," "#1"), guaranteed outcomes ("double your revenue"), and before/after comparisons without disclaimers. If running fintech ads, X requires financial services certification — complete it in X Business Center under "Ad Features."

**Problem: CPM is very high ($15+) and delivery is slow**
Solution: Your audience is too narrow. X requires minimum 10,000 users per audience segment for reliable delivery. Loosen targeting by removing 1-2 constraints (e.g., drop age range or device targeting), or expand follower lookalike list from 10 handles to 20+. For B2B audiences under 50K, increase bids 30% above suggested range — X's auction rewards higher bids when inventory is constrained.

**Problem: Clicks are happening but zero conversions on landing page**
Solution: Message mismatch between ad and landing page. The ad hook ("Your investor just asked for a 3-year model") should be the headline on your landing page, not your generic homepage value prop. Create a dedicated landing page per campaign theme. Also verify X Pixel is firing correctly (use X Pixel Helper Chrome extension) and check that your conversion window (view/click-through) is set appropriately for your sales cycle length.
