## Integration Tips

**Google Merchant Center (GMC):**
- Export feed optimization spec as CSV; use Supplemental Feed in GMC to override title/description without touching source catalog
- Set up automated feed rules in GMC to apply title formula transformations at scale
- Use Google's Product Studio (GMC > Products > Product Studio) to auto-generate lifestyle images from product-only shots using Gemini AI

**Google Ads Editor:**
- Bulk-upload Performance Max asset groups using the CSV template from Google Ads Editor v2.5+
- Create shared negative keyword lists and apply across all Shopping campaigns to prevent query cannibalization
- Use Automated Rules to pause products with ROAS < 1.0x for 14+ consecutive days

**Looker Studio / GA4 Integration:**
- Connect Merchant Center + Google Ads + GA4 to Looker Studio for unified product-level P&L view
- Build a "Shopping Health Dashboard": impression share, price competitiveness score, feed disapprovals, ROAS by product tier — refresh daily
- Set GA4 custom events for: add_to_cart, begin_checkout, purchase — funnel each back to Shopping campaign UTMs

**Klaviyo / Email for Cart Recovery:**
- Use Google's Enhanced Conversions + Klaviyo cart abandonment flow in parallel — Google remarketing for display/Shopping, Klaviyo for email/SMS
- Suppress Google remarketing audiences for cart abandoners who receive Klaviyo recovery email within 2 hours (avoid double-messaging)

**Northbeam / Triple Whale / Rockerbox (MMM):**
- Import Google Shopping conversion data into MMM platform to understand true incrementality vs. organic
- Use 7-day incrementality tests (holdout groups) before scaling PMax budgets by > 50%

**Shopify Integration:**
- Use Google & YouTube app for Shopify to sync product catalog to Merchant Center automatically
- Append custom_label values from Shopify metafields: margin tier, bestseller badge, clearance flag
- Enable enhanced conversions via Shopify Google Sales Channel for better signal fidelity

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Performance Max cannibalization of brand search campaigns**
Solution: Create a "Brand Exclusion" Campaign Negative Keyword List with all exact-match brand terms ([brand name], [brand name] + product, misspellings). Apply this negative list to PMax at the campaign level. Verify using Search Terms report — brand terms should show < 10% of PMax conversions. If cannibalization persists, create a separate Brand Search Campaign at "High Priority" with full brand keyword coverage.

**Problem: ROAS drops sharply after moving from Target CPA to Target ROAS**
Solution: The bidding algorithm needs 50+ conversions per month (per campaign) to optimize Target ROAS reliably. If you have fewer conversions, stay on Target CPA or use a higher-funnel micro-conversion (add-to-cart or begin-checkout) as the primary conversion action during ramp. Set a ROAS target 20% below actual performance to give Smart Bidding room to explore, then tighten weekly. Avoid changing bids more than once per 7-day learning window.

**Problem: Product feed disapprovals blocking key SKUs from Shopping**
Solution: Run a weekly Merchant Center Diagnostics export. The top 5 disapproval reasons for DTC brands are: (1) mismatched price — sync feed price to Shopify real-time via API, not daily batch; (2) unavailable landing page — 301 redirects break feed, ensure all product URLs return 200; (3) missing GTIN — for branded products, use brand MPN as fallback; (4) adult content policy — skincare/supplement brands often trip this, submit for manual review; (5) shipping mismatch — update feed shipping attribute to match exact checkout shipping options. Resolve within 48 hours or the product loses all impression share during the window.
