## Troubleshooting

**Problem: My metrics look dramatically worse than the benchmarks — is the benchmark data wrong?**
Solution: Check your definitions first. "MQL" means different things in different orgs — some count every form fill, others require explicit intent signals. If your MQL-to-SQL rate is 5%, you likely have a very loose MQL definition. Recalculate with a tighter definition (e.g., "demo request or free trial sign-up only") and rerun the benchmark comparison. Also verify your segment match: SMB benchmarks (MQL-to-SQL 10-15%) are very different from Enterprise (20-30%).

**Problem: I don't have data for half these metrics — the prompt output is incomplete.**
Solution: Run the Quick Copy-Paste Version with only the metrics you have. The AI will tell you which missing metrics are causing the most diagnostic blind spots, and suggest how to start tracking them. Prioritize adding tracking for pipeline coverage and marketing-sourced pipeline % first — these are the two most important board-level indicators and can be calculated from any CRM in 15 minutes.

**Problem: The improvement roadmap feels too generic for my specific situation.**
Solution: Add more context in the Company Context section — specifically your top 3 competitors, your primary ICP job title, and your biggest lost-deal reason. The more specific your context, the more precise the root cause hypotheses and recommended experiments. Also paste in 3-5 verbatim quotes from lost-deal notes or customer calls to calibrate the AI's understanding of your market dynamics.
