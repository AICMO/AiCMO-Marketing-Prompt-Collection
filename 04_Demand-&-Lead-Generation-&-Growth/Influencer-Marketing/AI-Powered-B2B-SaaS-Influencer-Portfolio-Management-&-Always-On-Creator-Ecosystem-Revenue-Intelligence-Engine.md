## Integration Tips
- **HubSpot:** Create a custom "Influencer Source" contact property and "Creator Campaign" deal property; build a list view filtered by these fields for monthly reporting
- **Notion/Airtable:** Build your creator portfolio database as a relational database with creator profiles, deliverables tracker, performance metrics, and contract dates — set automated reminders 60 days before contract expiration

## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** A creator's engagement is high but pipeline attribution is near zero.
**Solution:** This usually means the creator's audience is engaged but not ICP-matched, or your UTM tracking has gaps. First, audit the audience demographics using LinkedIn/YouTube analytics — check if followers match your ICP job titles and company sizes. Second, verify UTM links in recent posts are working and being clicked. Third, add a self-reported attribution question to your demo form specifically asking about creator content. If ICP alignment is genuinely low after 90 days, move to Watch tier regardless of engagement numbers.

**Problem:** A creator you want to renew is threatening to raise rates significantly above your ROI ceiling.
**Solution:** Respond with data, not gut feel. Share your cost-per-influenced-opportunity analysis showing what you can justify based on pipeline ROI. Propose a performance-bonus structure that rewards them for pipeline over-performance — this aligns incentives and creates upside for them without committing to a fixed fee you can't justify. If they decline a performance model, their ceiling is effectively $[calculated maximum] based on your ROI target, and you should explore replacing them with a creator whose rates align to the value delivered.

**Problem:** Audience overlap is high (3+ creators reaching the same people) causing ICP fatigue.
**Solution:** Run an audience overlap analysis using LinkedIn's Creator Analytics or third-party tools like SparkToro. If overlap exceeds 40% between two creators, either differentiate their content angle (one educational, one social proof-focused) or replace the lower-performing creator with one reaching an adjacent but distinct ICP segment. Set a portfolio rule: no two creators can have >35% audience overlap in steady state.
