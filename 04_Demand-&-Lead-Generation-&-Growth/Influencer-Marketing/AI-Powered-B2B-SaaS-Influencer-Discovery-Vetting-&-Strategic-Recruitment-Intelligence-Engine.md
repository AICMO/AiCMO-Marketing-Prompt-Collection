## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Influencers with high follower counts but ICP audience overlap below 30%**
Solution: Reframe your discovery criteria — prioritize follower *quality signals* over quantity. Search for influencers who explicitly tag their audience ("I write for RevOps leaders," "this is for enterprise CROs") rather than broad professional creators. Use SparkToro audience overlap reports before sending any outreach to prevent wasted conversations.

**Problem: Influencers interested in partnership but unable to provide any past performance data**
Solution: Structure a lower-risk pilot deal first — one sponsored post with full UTM tracking and a 30-day evaluation window before committing to a full quarterly program. Require a performance clause: if UTM-attributed conversions fall below a minimum threshold, deal does not renew at full rate. This surfaces bad-faith actors quickly while giving genuine first-time sponsors a fair opportunity.

**Problem: Recruited influencers generate brand awareness impressions but zero pipeline attribution**
Solution: Audit the campaign brief for conversion intent. Most influencer brand awareness campaigns fail to pipeline because the call-to-action is too soft ("check it out") or the landing page experience doesn't match the influencer's audience promise. Co-create a bespoke landing page with the influencer's content framing, offer a practitioner-specific lead magnet (benchmark report, ROI calculator) rather than a generic demo, and add a dedicated Calendly link so that booking friction is eliminated. Retarget engaged viewers via LinkedIn Matched Audiences within 7 days of post publication.
