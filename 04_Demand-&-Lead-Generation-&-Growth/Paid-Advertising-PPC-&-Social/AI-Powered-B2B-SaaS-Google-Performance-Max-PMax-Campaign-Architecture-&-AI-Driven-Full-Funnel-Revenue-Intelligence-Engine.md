## Troubleshooting

**Problem: PMax is spending 70%+ on Display/YouTube, not Search**
Solution: This is the most common B2B PMax failure mode. Fix in order:
1. Strengthen custom intent audience signals — add 30+ high-intent B2B search queries
2. Upload a large Customer Match list (minimum 1,000 contacts) as a positive signal
3. Add account-level negative keyword list (block irrelevant consumer and off-topic terms)
4. Check that offline conversions are importing correctly — if PMax only sees top-of-funnel form fills, it optimizes for cheap volume (Display/Gmail)
5. If problem persists after 30 days: create a separate Standard Shopping exclusion brand campaign to protect your highest-value search terms and let PMax handle the rest

**Problem: PMax learning phase never ends — perpetual "Limited" status**
Solution: PMax requires a minimum of 30-50 conversions per month to exit learning. For low-volume B2B:
1. Add micro-conversions as secondary goals: content downloads, pricing page visits, chatbot engagement — this feeds the algorithm while primary conversions accumulate
2. Lower your target CPA temporarily by 40-50% to allow more volume, then tighten gradually
3. Consider using "Maximize Conversions" (no target) for 60 days before introducing any target — this prioritizes learning over efficiency
4. If demo volume is < 10/month, consider building monthly campaign data with Standard Search first for 90 days, then migrate to PMax with a populated conversion history

**Problem: PMax is cannibalizing Brand and existing Search campaigns**
Solution: PMax and Search campaigns compete in the same auction. Protect Brand performance:
1. Create a dedicated Brand campaign in Standard Search with exact match keywords — Google's systems will prioritize Standard Search over PMax for exact brand queries (this is documented policy)
2. Add your brand terms as account-level negative keywords for ALL PMax campaigns
3. Monitor Search Impression Share in Brand campaign — if declining after PMax launch, file a Google Ads support case and request brand exclusion confirmation
4. Add competitor brand terms as Standard Search exact match campaigns if conquest is a priority — gives you direct control over those high-intent queries that PMax may deprioritize
