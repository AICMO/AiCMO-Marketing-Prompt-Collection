**Campaign A — Cold Awareness (Top of Funnel):**
- Goal: Build brand recognition with ICP who don't know you yet
- Audience layers:
  - Interest targeting: Business software, SaaS, startup culture, sales/marketing tools, entrepreneurship
  - Behavior targeting: Small business owners, B2B decision-makers, recent app installers (CRM/productivity)
  - Exclude: Existing customers (CRM upload), current retargeting pools
- Creative strategy: Education-first, zero selling pressure. Hook on the PROBLEM, not the product. Treat TikTok like a B2B media company, not an ad channel.
- Creative formats:
  - "Did you know" stat format: Bold stat about the problem + brief context + soft CTA ("Follow for more")
  - "3 signs you need [category]" listicle: Text-on-screen format with voiceover, fast-paced
  - Talking head from executive/founder: Authentic, no production value required, 15-30 sec
- KPIs to track: CPM <$5 for tech audiences, 6-second view rate >15%, follower conversion rate

**Campaign B — Warm Consideration (Mid-Funnel):**
- Audience:
  - Video engagement: Anyone who watched 50%+ of awareness ads in last 30 days
  - Website visitors: Pixel retargeting (exclude demo/pricing page visitors — move them to conversion)
  - Email engagement list: Uploaded from MAP — synced monthly
- Creative strategy: Problem-solution bridge. Show the product briefly. Lead with a customer outcome.
- Creative formats:
  - Quick product demo (30 sec): Screen recording with voiceover, "here's what changed in 30 days"
  - Social proof format: "Company X went from [problem state] to [outcome] using [Product]"
  - Comparison format: "[Old way] vs [New way with Product]" — side-by-side or before/after
- KPIs: CPC <$2.50, landing page CVR >4%, video completion rate >40%

**Campaign C — Conversion Retargeting (Bottom of Funnel):**
- Audience:
  - Pricing page visitors (last 30 days) — highest intent
  - Demo page visitors who didn't convert (last 14 days)
  - Lead magnet downloaders who haven't requested demo (last 60 days)
  - Lookalike: 1% lookalike of closed-won customers (upload from CRM quarterly)
- Creative strategy: Remove friction. Address the specific objection that's blocking conversion. Create urgency without desperation.
- Creative formats:
  - Direct response: "Still thinking about [Product]? Here's what to know before you decide."
  - Objection bust: "[Most common objection]? We built exactly for that." + proof point
  - Limited-offer format: Time-boxed trial, onboarding call offer, or free audit
- KPIs: CPL <target CPL, lead quality score (MQL rate from TikTok leads), demo show rate

**Campaign D — Spark Ads (Organic Amplification):**
- Source: Top-performing organic posts from company account OR creator partner posts (with authorization)
- Selection criteria: Posts with >5% engagement rate, ICP-relevant comment section, problem-aware hook
- Spark Ads allow you to run paid promotion behind organic-looking content — highest trust signal on TikTok
- Targeting: Mirror Campaign A audiences (cold ICP) + creator's own engaged following
- Budget: Keep to 10% — this is a multiplier on earned social, not a primary demand channel

DELIVERABLE 2 — CREATIVE FRAMEWORK & SCRIPT TEMPLATES:

**The B2B TikTok Creative Formula:**
Structure every ad using this sequence:
1. HOOK (0-3s): Scroll-stopping opening. Options: Bold claim ("Most [job title]s are losing X%"), Direct address ("If you're a [ICP role], this is for you"), Provocative question ("What if your [problem] wasn't actually a [problem]?")
2. PROBLEM AGITATION (3-10s): Make the pain feel real. Use specifics — numbers, named processes, relatable scenarios. Avoid generic.
3. SOLUTION DEMO (10-22s): Show the product in context. Not a feature tour — a BEFORE/AFTER moment. "Instead of [old way], now [new way]."
4. SOCIAL PROOF (22-27s): One proof element — customer name/logo, specific result ("3x pipeline in 60 days"), or a recognizable company using the product.
5. CTA (27-30s): Single action. "Try free at [domain]" or "Book a 15-min demo — link in bio." Never multiple CTAs.

**Script Template — Awareness (Problem Hook):**
Hook: "The average [ICP] spends [X hours] per week doing [painful task]."
Agitation: "And most don't even realize there's a better way — or they've tried [alternative] and got burned."
Solution: "That's why we built [Product]. [Screen recording: 5-second WOW moment — fastest path to AHA]."
Proof: "[Company logo] cut [task time] by 60% in their first month."
CTA: "Try [Product] free — link in bio."
Duration: 28-32 seconds
Format: Talking head + screen overlay (split screen)

**Script Template — Conversion (Objection Buster):**
Hook: "Still on the fence about [Product]? Totally fair. Here's what most people get wrong about us."
Agitation: "Most people assume [common objection]. And that used to be true — until we [product decision that solved it]."
Solution: "Now [specific customer type] can [specific capability] in [time frame]. [30-second product screen recording]."
Proof: "[3 company logos or 1 recognizable name] made the switch in under a week."
CTA: "Book a 15-min demo — no pressure, just answers. Link in bio."
Duration: 40-55 seconds (allowed for conversion-stage content)
Format: Founder or CS lead talking head — high authenticity, low production value

DELIVERABLE 3 — LEAD CAPTURE & QUALIFICATION ARCHITECTURE:

**Option A: TikTok Instant Forms (recommended for volume)**
- Reduces friction — user data pre-populated from TikTok profile
- Add 2-3 disqualifying questions: company size, role seniority, current tool stack
- Sync directly to HubSpot/Salesforce via TikTok Lead Gen native integration or Zapier
- Set up automatic email sequence triggered within 5 minutes of form submission
- Qualification flow: Lead form → CRM → Lead scoring → If MQL threshold met → Immediate SDR ping or AI SDR sequence

**Option B: Landing Page Traffic (recommended for quality)**
- Send clicks to a dedicated TikTok landing page — mobile-first, loads in <2 seconds
- Landing page structure: Above fold (product screenshot + single value prop + CTA), Social proof section (3 logos + 1 stat), Simple form (first name, work email, company, role), Optional: Video testimonial embed
- Install TikTok Pixel for full-funnel conversion tracking back to the ad level
- A/B test: Dedicated TikTok LP vs existing demo page — expect 2-4x higher CVR on dedicated page

**Lead Qualification Gate (add to all form types):**
- Question 1: "How many people are on your [relevant team]?" → Filter out <5 people (solo operators, consumers)
- Question 2: "What's your company's annual revenue / employee count?" → Filter by ICP segment
- Question 3: "What are you currently using for [category]?" → Intent signal + competitor intel
- Scoring: All three answers feed into lead score. Auto-route score >70 to SDR, score 40-69 to nurture sequence, score <40 to newsletter opt-in only.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: High volume, low ICP match rate (lots of consumer/small business leads)**
- Root cause: Audience too broad or interest targeting misconfigured
- Fix: Add company size qualifier to Instant Form (filter <10 employees at form level). Tighten interest targeting — remove general "entrepreneurship" in favor of specific software interests (CRM software, sales enablement, revenue operations). Build a negative audience from your "disqualified leads" CRM list and exclude it.

**Problem: Strong view rates but low CTR and lead volume**
- Root cause: Hook is working but offer or CTA isn't compelling enough for B2B buyers
- Fix: Test two CTA variants — low-friction ("Download free guide") vs high-intent ("Book 15-min demo"). For B2B, a lead magnet CTA typically generates 3-5x more volume at lower quality; demo CTA generates fewer but higher-intent leads. Run both in separate ad sets to find the right CPL/quality tradeoff for your sales team's capacity.

**Problem: Creative fatigue — performance drops sharply after week 3**
- Root cause: TikTok algorithm shows same creative to same user multiple times; B2B audiences are smaller and exhaust faster than B2C
- Fix: Never run more than 3-4 ad creatives per ad set simultaneously. Set frequency caps at the campaign level (max 2 impressions per user per week for awareness). Batch-produce 8-10 creatives per month and rotate in new creatives every 10-14 days. Prioritize hook variations (same body, different first 3 seconds) — the hook is the highest-leverage creative variable on TikTok.
