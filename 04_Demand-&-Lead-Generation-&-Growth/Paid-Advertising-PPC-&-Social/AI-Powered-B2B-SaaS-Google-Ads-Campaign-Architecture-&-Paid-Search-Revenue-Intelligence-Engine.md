## Troubleshooting

**Problem: CPL is 2x+ target, campaigns burning budget with few conversions**
Solution: Audit search term reports — if >30% of impressions are on irrelevant queries, expand negatives immediately. Check Quality Scores: if <5 on key terms, landing pages likely don't match search intent. Pause broad match keywords. Move budget to exact match only until account has conversion data. Verify conversion tracking is firing correctly (check Google Tag Assistant).

**Problem: Smart bidding underperforming after transition from manual CPC**
Solution: Smart bidding requires minimum 50 conversions in 30 days per campaign to stabilize. If under this threshold, switch back to Manual CPC + Enhanced CPC, or consolidate campaigns to pool conversion data. Add micro-conversions (pricing page visit, 3+ min sessions) as secondary conversion signals to feed the algorithm faster. Give smart bidding 2-4 weeks to exit "learning" phase before evaluating.

**Problem: Competitor campaigns getting disapproved or Quality Scores tanking**
Solution: Trademark policies prohibit using competitor brand names in ad copy — only in keywords. Ensure competitor brand names only appear in keyword targeting, not headlines or descriptions. Use phrases like "Alternative to [category]", "Switch from [pain point]", or "[Your brand] vs [generic category]" in ad copy. For low Quality Scores on competitor campaigns, the fix is dedicated landing pages (not homepage): a /vs/[competitor] page with direct comparison content matching search intent.
