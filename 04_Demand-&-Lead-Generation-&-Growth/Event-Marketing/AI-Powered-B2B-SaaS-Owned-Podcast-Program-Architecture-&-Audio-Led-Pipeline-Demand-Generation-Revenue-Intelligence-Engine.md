- Episode Production Tracker: status board with columns: Scheduled → Recorded → Edited → Published → Distributed

- Configure Riverside project with automatic cloud backup, AI noise cancellation, separate audio tracks per speaker

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Guest acceptance rate is below 20% for Tier 1 targets.**
Fix: Your outreach is likely too product-forward or generic. Rewrite Touch 1 to be purely relationship-focused with no mention of your product — the invitation is the ask, not a sales pitch. Add a "proof of concept" element: send the guest a link to your best existing episode as social proof of show quality. For cold Tier 1 targets, use a mutual connection warm intro rather than cold email (your CS team likely has relationships with customers who know your target guests).

**Problem: Downloads are stuck below 150/episode after 8 episodes.**
Fix: Distribution is likely failing, not content quality. Audit your guest amplification rate: are guests actually sharing episodes? If yes but downloads are low, your SEO-optimized episode titles aren't matching search intent — rewrite episode titles in the format "[Problem/Question] with [Expert Name] from [Recognizable Company]." Also audit your newsletter subscriber list — are you sending episode announcements to a list with >30% ICP match? If your newsletter list is small, run a LinkedIn targeted post campaign for $500-1000 to promote your top 3 episodes to your exact ICP.

**Problem: Self-reported podcast attribution is <2% of inbound demos after 90 days.**
Fix: Either your podcast isn't driving discovery yet (normal before 6 months and 500+ downloads/episode) or your self-reported attribution question is buried. Move "How did you hear about us?" to the top of your demo request form (first field, required), not the bottom where completion rates drop. Also expand your attribution survey beyond just forms: add a Calendly intake question to every demo booking page. Give it 6 months before concluding podcasting isn't working — B2B podcast influence operates on a 3-6 month lag as listeners build familiarity before taking action.
