## Troubleshooting

**Issue 1: LinkedIn ROAS looks terrible but we know it's driving awareness.**
- Root cause: Last-touch attribution doesn't capture LinkedIn's role as a research-and-awareness channel. LinkedIn rarely gets credit for deals that close via branded search or direct visit.
- Fix: In HubSpot/Salesforce, run a report on "Deals influenced by LinkedIn" (Campaign Influence object in SFDC, or HubSpot's multi-touch attribution report). Compare this to LinkedIn's "attributed" pipeline. The gap is your missing credit. Adjust your LinkedIn efficiency calculation manually using this influenced pipeline figure.

**Issue 2: Non-branded Google Search CPL is good but leads don't convert downstream.**
- Root cause: Keyword targeting is too broad — capturing top-of-funnel intent (e.g., "HR software") vs. bottom-of-funnel intent (e.g., "HR onboarding software for mid-market companies"). Also check: landing page ICP mismatch, offer-audience alignment, form friction.
- Fix: Segment non-branded campaigns by keyword intent tier. Analyze SQL rate by intent tier (informational vs. commercial vs. transactional). Kill or reduce budget on informational keywords with <3% SQL rate. Run a Gong call review on "non-branded search" leads that went dark after demo to diagnose ICP mismatch.

**Issue 3: Our attribution model shows $1M in paid pipeline but CRM shows only $400K in influenced deals.**
- Root cause: UTM parameter breakage (traffic from mobile app, ad click doesn't preserve UTM to CRM), form submissions not passing UTM data, or CRM campaign association is not configured correctly.
- Fix: Audit your UTM-to-CRM pipeline: (1) Manually test 5 ad clicks to confirm UTMs reach CRM contact record. (2) Check your landing page forms — ensure hidden UTM fields are present and populated. (3) In HubSpot, check "Original Source Drill-Down 1" field on 20 recent MQLs from paid channels — if it shows "Direct Traffic" instead of a UTM, you have attribution leakage. Fix with a JavaScript snippet that reads URL parameters and writes them to localStorage before form submission.
