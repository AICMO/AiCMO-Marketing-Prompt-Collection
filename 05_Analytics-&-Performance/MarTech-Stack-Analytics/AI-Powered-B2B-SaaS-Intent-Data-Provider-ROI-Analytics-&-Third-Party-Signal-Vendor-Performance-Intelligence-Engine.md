## Troubleshooting

**Problem: No intent attribution data in CRM — can't calculate vendor ROI**
*Solution:* Start with a proxy analysis: pull all accounts that booked meetings or created opportunities in last 90 days, then manually cross-reference against your intent vendor lists. For future quarters, this prompt will recommend the exact CRM field configuration and Zapier/API workflow to tag intent attribution automatically at the account and opportunity level.

**Problem: Signal volume is high but meetings booked is very low (< 5% of signaling accounts)**
*Solution:* This is almost always a signal activation failure, not a data quality failure. Check: (1) Outreach lag — are SDRs reaching out in 1-5 days or 15-30 days after signal? (2) Personalization gap — are outreach templates referencing the intent signal topic? (3) Outreach saturation — are your SDRs already sequences these accounts with generic cadences? The prompt's Phase 1 Signal Science analysis will diagnose the specific cause.

**Problem: Vendor overlap is high — hard to attribute which vendor "wins" the credit**
*Solution:* Use a "first signal wins" attribution rule for this analysis with a 30-day lookback window. Flag any account where 2+ vendors both signaled within the same 30-day window as "multi-signal overlap" — treat these as your highest-confidence accounts. The prompt will calculate overlap as a percentage of total coverage and model what your ROI looks like if you eliminated the lower-performing overlapping vendor.
