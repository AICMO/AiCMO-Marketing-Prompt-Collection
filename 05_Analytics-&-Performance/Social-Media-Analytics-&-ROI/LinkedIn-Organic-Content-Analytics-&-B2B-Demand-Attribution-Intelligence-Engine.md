## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Self-reported LinkedIn attribution is 3-4x higher than UTM-tracked LinkedIn traffic — which number do I report?**
Report both with context. The UTM gap is real and expected: most B2B buyers read LinkedIn on mobile, never click links, and then search directly or type your URL later. Best practice: report UTM-tracked as the "hard floor," self-reported as the "directional signal," and triangulate dark funnel using a 3-5x multiplier on UTM clicks as the "influenced estimate." Coach your sales team to always ask "where did you first hear about us?" on discovery calls and log this in CRM as a secondary source field — this is your most reliable dark funnel data.

**Problem: High follower growth but zero pipeline movement — LinkedIn feels like a vanity channel.**
Diagnose with the Audience Quality Audit first: what % of your followers match ICP? If it's below 25%, you have an audience-content mismatch — your content is attracting the wrong people (often marketing peers, not buyers). Fix: run 30 days of hyper-specific ICP-signal content (name their exact job title, their exact pain, their specific industry context) even if it reduces total engagement. Lower impressions from the right people outperforms high impressions from the wrong people every time. Also check: are your CTAs designed to capture demand or just build awareness? Add low-friction demand capture (DM me, comment "X" and I'll send you the framework) to every fifth post.

**Problem: CEO/executive is a strong writer but won't post consistently — content strategy collapses without them.**
Build a ghost-writing infrastructure: schedule a 30-minute monthly "content mining" conversation where you extract 8-10 story seeds, opinions, and frameworks from the executive's recent experiences. Convert these into post drafts for approval. Use a shared Notion doc or Slack channel where executives can drop raw thoughts (voice memo → transcript → draft) throughout the month. Critically: show the executive the attribution data — when they see "$432K pipeline influenced by your last 8 posts," consistency becomes a business priority, not a burden.
