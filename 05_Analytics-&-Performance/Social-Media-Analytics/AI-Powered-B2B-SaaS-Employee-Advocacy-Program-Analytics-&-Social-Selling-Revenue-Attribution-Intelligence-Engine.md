## Troubleshooting

**Problem: UTM data is showing "direct" or "(not set)" in CRM for advocacy-shared links**

Root cause: Advocates are copy-pasting URLs from the advocacy platform instead of clicking the platform's native share button, stripping UTM parameters in the process. Fix: (1) Set up a mandatory UTM enforcement rule in your advocacy platform if it supports it; (2) Create a Slack channel called #social-sharing-tips with a 60-second video showing how to share from the platform correctly; (3) Alternatively, use bit.ly or a custom short domain (share.yourcompany.com) that preserves UTMs regardless of how the link is shared.

**Problem: LinkedIn SSI scores don't correlate with pipeline in our data**

Root cause: SSI is a gameable metric — reps can increase their score by accepting all connection requests and posting any content, regardless of quality or audience relevance. If SSI is uncorrelated with pipeline in your data, pivot from SSI to a composite metric you control: (a) Monthly ICP-matching LinkedIn engagements (pull from Sales Navigator Team dashboard), (b) Content comment → DM → meeting booked rate, (c) Inbound inquiries that cited rep's LinkedIn content in their "how did you hear" response. These are harder to game and better predictors of social selling impact.

**Problem: Leadership wants to cut the advocacy program budget because "we can't prove it's working"**

Root cause: Most advocacy programs are measured on platform metrics (shares, impressions, engagement) rather than business metrics, making them easy to cut during budget reviews. Fix in 30 days: (1) Set up UTM tracking if not in place — 2-hour task with zero cost; (2) Add "employee LinkedIn content" as a self-reported attribution option on your demo request form; (3) Pull the last 90 days of closed-won deals and manually search each contact's LinkedIn activity against your top advocates' post history. Even finding 2-3 deals where a buyer clearly engaged with employee content before converting gives you a directional attribution story. Present this as: "We found $X in pipeline with a documented social touch in the prior 60 days. With proper instrumentation now in place, we project documenting $X × [3-4x factor] going forward."
