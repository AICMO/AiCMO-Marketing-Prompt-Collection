**Difficulty:** Advanced | **Time:** 20-30 min | **Tags:** b2b, social-listening, brand-intelligence, sentiment-analysis, competitive-intelligence, revenue-analytics, crisis-detection, voice-of-market, saas

## Overview
Continuously monitors brand mentions, competitor conversations, and category-level signals across social and web, synthesizes sentiment and share of voice into revenue-relevant intelligence, and routes actionable buyer signals directly into sales and marketing workflows. Use this when you need to turn the fire hose of social conversation into a structured early-warning system, competitive intelligence feed, and real-time demand-capture engine — without a full analyst team manually monitoring dashboards.

ANALYSIS FRAMEWORK REQUESTED:

**1. BRAND HEALTH SCORE & TREND ANALYSIS**

Calculate a composite Brand Health Score (0-100) using:
- Sentiment Score: (Positive mentions - Negative mentions) / Total mentions × 100 = Net Sentiment Score (weighted 40%)
- Share of Voice: Your brand mentions / (Your mentions + all competitor mentions) × 100 (weighted 30%)
- Mention Velocity: MoM mention growth rate vs. category growth rate (weighted 15%)
- Platform Distribution Quality: Premium platforms (LinkedIn, news) vs. low-signal platforms weighting (weighted 15%)

**Trend Signals to flag immediately:**
- Negative sentiment spike: >20% increase in negative mentions in any 7-day window → trigger crisis protocol
- Competitor surge: Any competitor growing mentions >30% MoM while you are flat or declining → flag as competitive threat
- Dark mention gap: Category problem keywords growing while brand mention share is flat → brand awareness gap in active buying population
- Viral negative post: Single post with >500 engagements carrying negative sentiment → requires executive response within 24h

**2. SHARE OF VOICE & COMPETITIVE INTELLIGENCE ANALYSIS**

**SOV Calculation:**
Total SOV = (Your brand mentions) / (Sum of all competitor mentions + your mentions) × 100

**Competitive Content Intelligence:**
For each competitor showing >20% MoM mention growth, extract:
a) What narrative or messaging is driving their conversation surge? (product launch, pricing change, executive hire, partnership, thought leadership campaign)
b) Which platforms are amplifying their growth? (LinkedIn vs. Reddit vs. G2 — different signals for different revenue responses)
c) Is their growth driven by positive sentiment (marketing working) or negative sentiment (product/service failures = your opportunity)?
d) Which ICP personas are most vocal about them? (identifies competitive targeting opportunities)

**Competitor Vulnerability Detection:**
Scan competitor negative sentiment themes for:
- Pricing complaints → draft competitive displacement messaging for sales team
- Feature gaps → create comparison content targeting those gaps
- Support/reliability issues → prepare migration content and SDR outreach sequences
- Layoffs or leadership changes → trigger champion-tracking alerts in CRM

**3. BUYER INTENT SIGNAL EXTRACTION & SCORING**

Intent Signal Framework — rank every captured social mention on a 3-tier buyer intent scale:

**Tier 1 — Active Buying Signal (route to SDR within 24h):**
- Explicit "looking for alternatives to [Competitor]" posts
- Comparison questions: "has anyone used [Your Company] vs. [Competitor]?" on LinkedIn, Reddit, Slack communities
- Pain point posts matching your ICP + product category, written by director-level or above at ICP-fit companies
- "Our contract with [Competitor] is up in [timeframe]" signals
- Job postings at ICP companies for roles your product enables (e.g., "Head of RevOps" posting = signal company is scaling revenue operations)

**Tier 2 — Research/Evaluation Signal (enroll in nurture, target with retargeting):**

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Listening tool generates hundreds of "mentions" per day but 80% are noise (bot accounts, irrelevant uses of your brand name, unrelated content)**
Fix: Refine your Boolean keyword queries with exclusion operators. For a company named "Beacon": instead of monitoring "Beacon" alone, use `"Beacon" AND ("data platform" OR "analytics" OR "your CEO name" OR "your product name") NOT ("lighthouse" OR "emergency beacon" OR "music festival")`. Invest 2-3 hours in query refinement in the first month — a precise query catching 50 real signals is 10x more valuable than a broad query generating 500 pieces of noise that the team ignores. Review query quality monthly and remove the top 3 sources of false positives.

**Problem: Tier 1 buyer intent signals are identified but SDRs don't follow up because the social context isn't visible in their CRM workflow**
Fix: The signal must arrive where SDRs already work — inside Salesforce or HubSpot, not in a separate Slack channel they can deprioritize. Build the integration so the listening signal creates a CRM task or lead record with the post text, author profile URL, and a pre-written personalized opener embedded in the task description. Remove any friction requiring the SDR to navigate to a separate tool. Track follow-up rate in CRM — if <50% of Tier 1 signals are contacted within 48h, the workflow has a friction point to diagnose.

**Problem: Social listening identifies competitor vulnerabilities but the marketing team can't act quickly enough to capitalize on them**
Fix: Pre-build the response assets before the signal fires. For each of your top 3 competitors, create a ready-to-deploy "competitor vulnerability kit": a comparison landing page targeting their known weaknesses, a 3-email SDR sequence for prospects who mention switching from that competitor, and a LinkedIn ad creative set with displacement messaging. Store these in a shared folder with the social listening team. When a competitor surge is detected, the activation timeline drops from 2 weeks (build from scratch) to 24-48 hours (deploy pre-built assets). Review and update the kits quarterly based on the latest competitive intelligence.
