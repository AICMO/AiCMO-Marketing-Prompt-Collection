## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Can't attribute podcast listens to specific contacts because podcast platforms don't share listener emails.**
Solution: Use a podcast-gated bonus resource strategy — offer a downloadable companion guide or episode transcript via an email capture form in show notes. Even capturing 8-12% of listeners creates a trackable cohort. Combine with LinkedIn pixel on your show notes pages and retargeting to create a probabilistic attribution model rather than relying on deterministic matching.

**Problem: Downloads are strong but pipeline attribution shows near zero — stakeholders are skeptical of podcast ROI.**
Solution: Run a "podcast listener survey" via a 2-question pop-up on your website asking "Have you listened to [Podcast Name]?" — typically reveals 3-5x more pipeline influence than CRM data captures. Also analyze closed-won deal transcripts (Gong) for all deals in the past 6 months for podcast mentions. B2B podcast influence is heavily under-attributed due to dark social consumption patterns.

**Problem: Episode-level completion data isn't available or inconsistent across platforms.**
Solution: Use show notes page dwell time (Google Analytics) as a proxy for episode engagement — listeners who read the full show notes tend to complete 70%+ of the episode. Cross-reference with your highest-social-share episodes (Buffer/Hootsuite analytics) to build a multi-signal engagement score per episode even without direct completion data.
