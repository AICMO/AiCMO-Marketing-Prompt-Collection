## Integration Tips

- **HubSpot:** Install LinkedIn Insight Tag via HubSpot's native integration → Settings > Integrations > LinkedIn. Create "LinkedIn Content Consumers" smart list based on page visits from `lnkd.in` referrer. Map LinkedIn-attributed contacts to deal pipeline in custom property.
- **Salesforce:** Use LinkedIn Sales Navigator + Salesforce integration to log LinkedIn touchpoints on leads/contacts. Create LinkedIn Engagement campaign influence report in Salesforce CRM Analytics.
- **Google Analytics 4:** Set up LinkedIn as a channel group (Source: linkedin.com / Medium: social). Create custom LinkedIn attribution reports in GA4 Explorations comparing LinkedIn-referred sessions to conversion events.
- **Looker/Tableau:** Connect LinkedIn Marketing Solutions API (OAuth 2.0) to pull Page Analytics, Follower Demographics, and Post Statistics endpoints. Build automated weekly dashboard with trend alerts.
- **Zapier/Make:** Automate LinkedIn post performance → Google Sheets log every 24h. Trigger Slack alert when any post hits 10K+ impressions within 6 hours (algorithm breakout signal).
- **LinkedIn API:** Use LinkedIn Marketing Developer Platform (Organization Pages API) to pull `organizationalEntityFollowerStatistics`, `shareStatistics`, and `organizationPageStatistics` endpoints for automated reporting.
- **Clay/Apollo:** Enrich LinkedIn-attributed leads with company firmographic data for ICP scoring before CRM handoff.
- **Gong/Chorus:** Cross-reference LinkedIn content engagement data with Gong call transcripts to identify whether LinkedIn-consumed content topics appear in sales discovery conversations.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: LinkedIn analytics don't match CRM attribution data — pipeline numbers are wildly different**
Solution: LinkedIn Insight Tag and CRM attribution use different attribution windows and models. LinkedIn's default is 30-day post-click, 7-day post-view. Configure HubSpot/Salesforce to use the same lookback window. Add UTM parameters (`?utm_source=linkedin&utm_medium=organic&utm_campaign=[post-topic]`) to all links in LinkedIn posts to enable CRM-side session tracking independent of LinkedIn's pixel.

**Problem: Audience demographics look right (VP+ HR) but posts aren't reaching them — most engagement is from peers and competitors**
Solution: This is LinkedIn's algorithm over-indexing on your existing engaged audience (often employees, partners, similar companies). To break out: (1) ask 5 ICP customers to comment on your next post within the first 30 minutes — LinkedIn uses early commenter profiles to determine wider distribution audience; (2) use LinkedIn Thought Leader Ads ($500-1,000 test) to amplify a high-performing post specifically to VP+ HR at 200-2,000 employee companies; (3) write content using language your ICP searches (e.g., "compensation benchmarking" vs. "pay equity") to trigger keyword-based discovery.

**Problem: Content performance data is available but can't connect it to actual pipeline — attribution gap**
Solution: Implement a 3-layer attribution stack: (1) UTM tracking on all LinkedIn links → GA4 → CRM source field; (2) LinkedIn Insight Tag for view-through attribution on your website; (3) manual LinkedIn touch logging in CRM — train SDRs to log LinkedIn profile views, InMail, and comment interactions as activities on the lead/contact record. Use a CRM workflow to auto-tag any lead where `Original Source = LinkedIn Organic` to build a cleaner attribution cohort for monthly reporting.
