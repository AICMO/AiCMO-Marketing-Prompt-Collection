## Integration Tips

- **TikTok Analytics Pro**: Upgrade to Business account to unlock re-watch data, audience demographics by content type, and follower activity time windows — critical inputs for this analysis
- **Google Analytics 4**: Create a custom channel grouping for "TikTok Organic" using source/medium = `tiktok / organic` and append UTM parameters `utm_source=tiktok&utm_medium=organic&utm_content=[video-topic]` to every bio link to separate organic from paid TikTok traffic
- **TikTok Shop**: Connect Shopify or WooCommerce via TikTok's native integration; enable product tagging on all product-adjacent videos to capture in-video purchase intent without requiring a bio link click
- **Triple Whale / Northbeam**: For DTC brands using multi-touch attribution platforms, configure TikTok organic as a separate channel with a custom attribution window (recommend 7-day post-view, 1-day post-click for organic) to see blended contribution in paid media models

- **Google Search Console**: Connect branded keyword search volume tracking to TikTok viral moments by exporting weekly branded query impressions and overlaying with your posting calendar to measure organic search halo effect

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: High follower count but consistently low views (stuck in follower bubble)**
Solution: Your account has trained the algorithm that your content only resonates with existing followers. Fix by: (1) Deleting or archiving the 20% lowest-performing videos that dragged down account-level completion rate signals. (2) Posting 3 consecutive videos using trending sounds from the top 50 on TikTok Creative Center in your niche — trending audio instantly expands FYP reach beyond your follower graph. (3) Engage aggressively in comments on top creators in your category for 7 days — this signals to the algorithm that your account is active in relevant interest clusters.

**Problem: High views but zero conversions or TikTok Shop sales**
Solution: Reach and revenue intent are decoupled — you're attracting entertainment audiences, not buyers. Audit: (1) Check if your top-viewed content ever includes a product mention, price anchor, or result-driven claim. Pure entertainment content converts at <0.05%; problem-solution content converts at 0.5-2%. (2) Add a TikTok Shop product link to every product-adjacent video — in-video CTAs convert 3-4x better than bio link clicks. (3) Create a dedicated conversion content type: 60-second "problem → product → result" videos with TikTok Shop CTA pinned from second 10 onward. Run these 2x/week alongside your high-reach content.

**Problem: Inconsistent performance — one viral video then silence**
Solution: You have viral content but no algorithm-trained category signal. Fix by: (1) Immediately after a viral video, post 3 consecutive videos in the same format, same content topic, and same sound category within 72 hours — capitalize on the algorithm's temporary interest-graph expansion. (2) Review what the viral video did differently in its first 3 seconds vs. your typical content and extract the hook formula. (3) Build a "content series" around the viral topic (Part 2, Part 3, "You asked so here's more about...") — series content gets 40-60% higher completion rates because TikTok recognizes returning viewers as a strong quality signal.
