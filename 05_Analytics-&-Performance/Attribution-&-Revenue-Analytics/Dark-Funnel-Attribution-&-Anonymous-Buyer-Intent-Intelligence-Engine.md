## Integration Tips

- **HubSpot:** Use Custom Properties to tag contacts with `dark_funnel_source` (G2 / Podcast / Community / LinkedIn Organic) and `intent_tier` (1/2/3). Build Active Lists for Tier 1 signals to auto-enroll in sequences. Use HubSpot's Campaign influence reporting to see dark-funnel-tagged contacts in pipeline.
- **Salesforce:** Add `Dark_Funnel_Signal__c` (multi-select picklist) and `Brand_Aware_Before_Outreach__c` (checkbox + date) fields to Opportunity. Build a Lightning Report comparing win rates and deal velocity across these segments. Use Einstein Analytics to correlate dark funnel exposure with revenue outcomes.
- **6sense / Bombora:** Sync "High Intent" account flags to Salesforce via native integration. Create 6sense Segment for "Dark Funnel Ready" (intent tier 1 + no open opp) and push to LinkedIn Campaign Manager for precision ABM ads.
- **RB2B / Koala / Clearbit Reveal:** Set up Slack webhook alerts: when a target account visits 3+ pages in a session with no CRM match → fires Slack message to SDR team with company name, pages visited, firmographic data, and suggested outreach angle.
- **G2 Buyer Intent:** Export weekly intent report → run through Python/Google Sheets VLOOKUP against your ICP account list → pipe matched accounts into Salesforce via Zapier or native G2/Salesforce connector. Upgrade to category-level intent to see competitive research.
- **Google Sheets Tracking Dashboard:** Build a weekly "Dark Funnel Scorecard" pulling: (1) G2 intent accounts, (2) IP-identified website accounts, (3) community members matched to CRM, (4) podcast download trend, (5) SDR outreaches triggered by dark funnel signals, (6) meetings booked, (7) pipeline created.
- **Zapier/Make Automation:** G2 intent alert → Salesforce account lookup → if no open opp → create Task for SDR + add to HubSpot sequence. Set up in under 2 hours with no engineering required.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Sales team dismisses dark funnel attribution as "soft metrics" and refuses to log dark funnel signals in CRM.**
Solution: Frame it as SDR efficiency, not marketing credit. Show the rep-level data: SDRs who respond to dark funnel signals (G2 intent + LinkedIn activity) are booking 2-3x more meetings than those doing pure cold outreach. Run a 30-day pilot with one SDR using dark funnel triggers and share the meeting-booked rate vs. their cold outbound baseline. Numbers convert skeptics.

**Problem: IP enrichment match rates are too low (<5%), making account-level identification unreliable.**
Solution: IP enrichment typically only identifies 10-20% of B2B visitors on desktop (drops to near zero on mobile). Supplement with: (1) Expand your gated content with low-friction lead capture (one-click LinkedIn sign-in via tools like Chilipiper or Metadata), (2) Run cookieless pixel via Koala or Common Room to capture LinkedIn profile data for visitors who click through from LinkedIn posts, (3) Focus IP enrichment on only your highest-traffic, highest-intent pages (pricing, comparison, case studies) rather than all pages.

**Problem: Post-sale attribution surveys have <20% response rates, making the revenue influence model statistically weak.**
Solution: Embed the buyer journey survey into the customer onboarding sequence (Week 2-3 when engagement is highest), keep it to 3 questions maximum, offer a small incentive (e.g., $25 Amazon gift card for completion), and have the CSM ask the top 5 questions verbally during the first QBR. Use Typeform or Jotform with HubSpot integration so responses auto-populate CRM fields. Target 50%+ response rate from Enterprise accounts with CSM-assisted delivery.
