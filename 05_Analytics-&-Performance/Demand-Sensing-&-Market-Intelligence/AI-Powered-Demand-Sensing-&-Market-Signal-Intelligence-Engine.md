## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Signal data is incomplete — only have 1-2 signal sources, not all of them**
Solution: Run the engine with whatever data you have; explicitly note "Confidence: Low" for segments where fewer than 3 signal types are available. Prioritize getting access to at least one hiring signal source (LinkedIn, Greenhouse job boards) and one technographic source (BuiltWith free tier, HG Insights trial) as the highest-ROI data investments. Even two signal types produce significantly better account prioritization than gut feel alone.

**Problem: The hot account list returns accounts that sales already knows about or has in active pipeline**
Solution: This is a calibration signal — add your existing pipeline accounts as an exclusion list in the prompt and re-run. The value here is net-new account identification. If too many overlap, you likely need broader ICP criteria — ask the engine to expand to Tier 2 signal thresholds or adjacent verticals you haven't fully penetrated.

**Problem: Demand signal scores don't correlate with actual pipeline velocity over time**
Solution: After each quarter, run a "signal retrospective" — compare which signal types best predicted actual close rates. Adjust the weighting model in the Advanced Version (hiring signals weight, funding weight, etc.) based on your historical data. Companies with longer sales cycles (6+ months) should weight regulatory/compliance signals higher since those are slower-moving but more predictable; companies with 30-60 day cycles should weight hiring and funding signals higher since they indicate immediate budget availability.
