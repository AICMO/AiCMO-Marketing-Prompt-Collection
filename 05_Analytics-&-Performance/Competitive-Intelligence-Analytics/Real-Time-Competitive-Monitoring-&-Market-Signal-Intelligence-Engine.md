## Troubleshooting

**"We're generating intelligence but sales isn't using it"**
The fix is format, not volume. Sales teams ignore long reports. Restructure output as a 1-page "Competitive Flash" with three sections: What changed, Why it matters, What to say on your next call. Distribute via Slack, not email. Include a specific script line they can use immediately.

**"Signal noise is overwhelming the real threats"**
Apply a two-filter system: (1) ICP relevance — does this affect our target customer segment? (2) Timing urgency — does this require action in <30 days? Only signals passing both filters escalate to Priority 1. Everything else goes into a monthly digest. Aim for no more than 3-5 Priority 1 alerts per monitoring cycle.

**"Competitor moves too fast — our response is always late"**
The lag is usually in the response chain, not the detection. Pre-authorize three "rapid response" actions your team can execute without approval: (1) publish a comparison blog post, (2) launch a targeted LinkedIn campaign to competitor customers, (3) send a competitive briefing email to at-risk customers. When a Priority 1 alert fires, execution starts in 24 hours, not after three rounds of review.
