## Troubleshooting

**Problem: "The output is too generic and doesn't reflect my actual data"**
Fix: Paste actual numbers from GA4 reports rather than describing them. Include the GA4 Acquisition Overview table and at least 5 specific page-level metrics. The more specific the input, the more specific the diagnosis. Use GA4 → Reports → Acquisition → Traffic Acquisition, copy the table data directly.

**Problem: "The revenue calculations seem off or unrealistic"**
Fix: Verify your AOV/ACV and close rate inputs are accurate for your business. For B2B, ensure you're using pipeline value (not closed revenue) to make the math meaningful. Add your historical demo-to-close rate and average sales cycle length to improve accuracy of revenue projections.

**Problem: "I don't have GA4 data in the right format"**
Fix: Use GA4's built-in report sharing: Reports → share icon → copy link, then describe the key numbers you see. Alternatively, export any GA4 report to Google Sheets (Reports → download icon → Google Sheets) and paste the relevant rows directly into the prompt. Even partial data produces actionable insights — start with your top 5 landing pages and main conversion funnel.
