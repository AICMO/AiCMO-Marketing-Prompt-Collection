## Overview
This engine benchmarks your B2B marketing and sales funnel conversion rates against industry standards, identifies underperforming stages using statistical gap analysis, and generates AI-powered optimization playbooks for each funnel transition — from first touch to closed-won. Use it when pipeline generation is flat despite channel spend, when sales complains about lead quality, or during quarterly business reviews to diagnose revenue leaks.

## Integration Tips

**HubSpot / Salesforce:**
- Build custom reports that track each funnel stage conversion rate with 30/60/90-day rolling windows. Create saved filters by source, ICP segment, and deal size so you can run this analysis by cohort.
- Set up automated Slack notifications via HubSpot Workflows or Salesforce Flow when any stage conversion drops below your defined alert thresholds.
- Use HubSpot's "Contact Property Trends" or Salesforce Einstein Analytics to automate weekly benchmark comparison emails to the RevOps team.

**Gong / Chorus / Clari:**
- Feed deal progression data directly into this prompt by exporting stage velocity reports from Gong Forecast or Clari. The "deal health score" distributions by stage provide rich root cause data.
- Use Gong's "Initiative Tracker" to tag calls where specific objections arise at each stage — this feeds the competitive intelligence and root cause modules.

**Tableau / Looker / Google Data Studio:**
- Build a live funnel waterfall dashboard with benchmark overlay lines. Connect to your CRM via direct connector or data warehouse. Schedule weekly automated PDF exports to the CMO and revenue leadership.
- Create a "funnel cohort view" showing how conversion rates change by MQL creation month — critical for identifying whether recent campaigns are generating higher or lower quality leads.

**Marketo / Pardot:**
- Use program-level performance reports to segment MQL-to-SQL rates by campaign source. This is the fastest way to identify which specific demand gen programs are generating high-quality vs. low-quality leads.
- Set up progressive profiling forms to capture ICP qualification data passively over multiple visits — this improves MQL quality without reducing form fill rates.

**Excel / Google Sheets:**
- Build a simple benchmarking template using the stage metrics above with conditional formatting to flag stages Red/Yellow/Green. Paste this prompt output alongside your funnel data for a monthly review deck.
- Create a rolling 13-week funnel conversion tracker to visualize trend lines for each stage. Funnel conversion tends to be noisy week-to-week but directional trends emerge clearly at 4-6 weeks.

## Troubleshooting

**Issue: "I don't have conversion rate data for all stages — my CRM tracking is incomplete."**
Solution: Start with what you have. Even 3-4 data points produce useful benchmarking. For missing stages, ask the prompt to produce benchmarks and hypotheses based on the adjacent metrics you do have. Then use the output to build the business case for implementing proper funnel tracking (UTMs, lifecycle stage automation in HubSpot/Marketo, deal stage definitions in Salesforce) before the next quarterly review.

**Issue: "The benchmarks don't feel right for my business — our ACV and motion are unusual."**
Solution: Add additional context in the Company Profile section: multi-product vs. single-product, average number of stakeholders in the buying process, industry-specific buying cycles (e.g., "Our buyers are government agencies with 9-12 month procurement cycles"). The more context you give, the more accurate the benchmark adjustment. You can also explicitly ask the prompt: "Adjust the benchmark framework for a [specific context] company and explain the adjustments."

**Issue: "The optimization playbook output is too generic — I need it tailored to our specific situation."**
Solution: The more real data you provide in the input, the more specific the output. Include actual CRM field definitions, your ICP criteria, recent campaign names, specific competitor names, and the top 3 reasons sales rejects MQLs. The prompt performs at its best with complete, specific inputs. Consider running Module 3 (Root Cause Diagnosis) separately with maximum context before running the full analysis.
