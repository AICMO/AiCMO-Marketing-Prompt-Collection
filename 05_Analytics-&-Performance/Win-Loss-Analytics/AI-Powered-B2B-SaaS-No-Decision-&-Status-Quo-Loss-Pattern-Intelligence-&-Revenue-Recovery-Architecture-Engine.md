## Troubleshooting

**Issue: CRM close notes are too vague to categorize losses accurately.**
Fix: Implement a structured close survey — when a rep marks a deal closed-lost, a 5-question form auto-triggers in Salesforce/HubSpot requiring: (1) Loss type selection, (2) Primary decision-maker who killed deal, (3) Competitor status, (4) Was there a compelling event at any point?, (5) Is deal recoverable in 6 months? This data populates the no-decision matrix automatically. Takes 2 weeks to implement; delivers clean data within 30 days.

**Issue: Champions are unresponsive to reactivation outreach — open rates under 15%.**
Fix: Switch from product-led to insight-led reactivation. Do not mention your product in the first email. Instead, send a personalized insight relevant to their current business challenge (use LinkedIn activity, recent press, or industry report as the hook). Reference their specific situation from prior conversations. The goal of Email 1 is a reply, not a demo. Product relevance enters at Email 2 or when they respond.

**Issue: Economic buyer engagement rate is low — deals are champion-only throughout the cycle.**
Fix: Deploy the "executive briefing request" technique at Stage 2. After the champion shows strong interest, the rep says: "In my experience, deals that have executive visibility move 40% faster. Would it be possible to do a 20-minute executive briefing — I'll prepare a one-pager so it's a minimal ask on their time?" This gets the economic buyer in the room early, surfaces budget conversations earlier, and reduces Type 2/3/4 losses by 20–30%.
