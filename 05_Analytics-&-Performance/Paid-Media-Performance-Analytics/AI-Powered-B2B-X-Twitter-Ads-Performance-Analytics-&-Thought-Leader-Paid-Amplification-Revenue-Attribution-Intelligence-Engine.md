- **HubSpot**: Create a X/Twitter Ads source property in HubSpot; use UTM source=twitter&medium=paid-social&campaign={{campaign_name}} to auto-attribute contacts. Build a saved filter for "Original Source = Paid Social" + "Latest Source Drill-Down 1 = twitter" to isolate X pipeline

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: X Ads pixel conversions are far lower than actual CRM form fills from X traffic**
Solution: Audit UTM parameter consistency across all X Ads — a missing or malformed UTM on even one high-traffic ad can cause significant under-attribution. Check that the X pixel's conversion event fires on the thank-you page specifically (not the landing page). Also verify no ad blockers or browser privacy settings are suppressing pixel fires (typically 15–25% under-count is normal; > 40% gap suggests a pixel implementation error).

**Problem: Thought Leader Ads engagement rate looks strong but CPL is high with zero MQLs**
Solution: The amplified content is likely not optimized for conversion — strong engagement with thought leadership content often drives saves and follows rather than link clicks to a conversion-optimized page. Add a targeted CTA in the reply thread of the promoted post linking to a relevant gated asset or demo landing page. Alternatively, use a Conversation Card ad format to capture leads directly within X rather than requiring an outbound click.

**Problem: Keyword targeting campaigns have high spend and impressions but very low click-through rate (< 0.10%)**
Solution: Keyword targeting on X captures users based on their recent engagement with content containing those keywords — not search intent. The audience is scrolling a social feed, not in an active research mindset. Shift from direct response copy to native-style, value-first content that earns engagement before asking for the click. Test educational carousel posts (swipe-to-learn format) which generate 2–3x higher engagement rates than single-image direct response ads in keyword-targeted B2B campaigns.
