# Email Marketing Performance Analytics & Revenue Intelligence Engine - Full-Funnel Email Program Analysis & Optimization

**Difficulty:** Advanced | **Time:** 20-30 min | **Tags:** email, analytics, revenue, automation, b2b, b2c, deliverability, optimization

## Overview
Analyzes your email marketing program's full-funnel performance — from deliverability and list health through open/click/conversion rates to attributed revenue — and produces a prioritized optimization roadmap with specific, executable recommendations. Use this when you need to diagnose why email performance is stalling, justify email investment to leadership, or find the highest-leverage improvements in your email program.

**Klaviyo:**
- Export your Klaviyo Analytics → Benchmarks report and paste directly into the Advanced prompt

- Pull predictive analytics (CLV, churn risk) from Klaviyo's AI features and include in the segmentation section

**Salesforce Marketing Cloud:**
- Use Intelligence Reports (formerly Datorama) for cross-campaign performance aggregation

## Troubleshooting

**Issue: AI gives generic benchmark numbers that don't match my industry**
Fix: Specify your exact vertical and business model in the prompt (e.g., "B2B SaaS with 14-day free trial, average ACV $8,400, enterprise buyers" vs. "B2C subscription, $49/month, direct consumer"). The more specific your context, the more accurate the benchmarking. If you know your specific industry benchmarks, include them explicitly and ask the AI to use your numbers instead.

**Issue: Revenue attribution estimates seem too high/low**
Fix: Email revenue attribution is genuinely complex — most ESPs default to "last-click" attribution which over-credits email. Clarify your attribution model in the prompt (last-click / first-click / linear / data-driven). If using multi-touch attribution, import your attribution data from your analytics platform rather than relying on ESP-native numbers. Tell the AI which model you're using and it will calibrate estimates accordingly.

**Issue: Recommendations are too broad — "improve subject lines" without specifics**
Fix: Add 5-10 of your actual subject lines (best and worst performers with open rates) to the Advanced prompt. The AI needs examples to give you specific, actionable copy direction. Include your brand voice guidelines and any constraints (e.g., "we never use all-caps or exclamation points"). The more raw material you provide, the more precise the output.
