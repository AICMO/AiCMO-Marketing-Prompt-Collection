## Troubleshooting

**Problem: "I don't have CRM pipeline attribution data for organic search — my attribution model doesn't track source."**
Solution: Start with a proxy model. In HubSpot, pull contacts whose Original Source is "Organic Search" and trace their associated deals. In Salesforce, use Lead Source field. Even imperfect last-touch attribution establishes a baseline. Run the prompt with this proxy data and note the attribution model limitation — the output will still surface high-value keyword and content insights.

**Problem: "The prompt output says my organic traffic is underperforming but my team thinks traffic is up 30% YoY."**
Solution: Traffic volume and pipeline contribution are two different metrics. High TOFU traffic without BOFU content coverage produces exactly this result: lots of visitors, minimal MQLs. Feed in your keyword segmentation by funnel stage — the prompt will show that TOFU traffic (informational keywords) is growing while BOFU coverage (buyer-intent keywords) remains thin. This is the most common B2B SEO misalignment and the most important insight this prompt surfaces.

**Problem: "The competitor gap analysis shows 40+ keywords I should target — how do I prioritize?"**
Solution: Filter by two criteria: (1) keyword funnel stage — BOFU first, MOFU second, TOFU last; (2) your current domain authority vs. the ranking competitor. If a competitor has 30+ more Domain Rating points than you, ranking above them for that keyword in 90 days is unlikely. Filter to keywords where the ranking competitors have similar or lower authority than you, AND the keyword shows clear BOFU intent. This will reduce a 40-keyword list to 5–8 genuinely executable opportunities.
