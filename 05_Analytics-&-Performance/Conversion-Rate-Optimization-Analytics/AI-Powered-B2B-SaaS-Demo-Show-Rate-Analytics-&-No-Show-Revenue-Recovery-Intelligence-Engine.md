## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Show rate calculation is inconsistent — different numbers from different reports.**
Fix: Standardize the definition. A "demo" counts as attended only when the prospect (not AE alone) joins for ≥5 minutes. Create a single Salesforce report with this definition and treat it as the single source of truth. Audit whether AEs are logging "no-shows" correctly or marking them as "completed" to protect their stats.

**Problem: No-Show Risk Score predicts poorly in early weeks — too many false positives.**
Fix: The score needs calibration against your actual data. For the first 30 days, log predicted scores against actual outcomes. Identify which signals are most predictive for your ICP (booking lag is usually the strongest). Adjust weights in month 2. Don't use the score for hard gates (blocking calendar access) until you have 60 days of calibration data.

**Problem: AEs aren't following the T+10min call protocol after no-shows — recovery rate stays flat.**
Fix: Remove the manual dependency. Use a Salesloft/Outreach automation to send the "We missed you" email automatically when Demo Status is set to No-Show — AE doesn't need to trigger it. For the phone call, create an automated high-priority Task in CRM that fires immediately; track completion rate in AE dashboards. Make call attempt rate a coaching metric, not just show rate.
