## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Attribution data shows <50% of pipeline as marketing-attributed, making MarOps look ineffective**
*Root cause:* Often a UTM parameter gap (forms not capturing source), CRM campaign association missing, or multi-touch attribution model not configured.
*Fix:* Audit the last 30 closed-won deals manually — trace back to first marketing touch. If majority had a marketing interaction not captured, the issue is technical (UTM/integration), not actual performance. Run a UTM audit: filter all opportunities created in last 90 days, check Campaign Source field. Anything blank = attribution gap.

**Problem: MQL→SQL conversion rate is low but sales says lead quality is fine**
*Root cause:* Disconnect between marketing's MQL definition and sales qualification criteria. Often MQL scoring is too permissive (behavioral signals over intent signals) or sales team is not logging rejections correctly.
*Fix:* Run a cohort analysis: pull all MQLs from last 180 days, segment by MQL score band, and track SQL conversion by band. If high-score MQLs don't convert better than low-score MQLs, the scoring model is broken. If they do, the threshold is too low. Schedule a sales-marketing MQL calibration session with 20-deal audit.

**Problem: Database enrichment coverage is low (<50%) but enrichment tool is in the stack**
*Root cause:* Most enrichment platforms require manual batch uploads or webhook triggers that weren't configured at setup. Or the enrichment is running but on wrong fields (enriching "Company Name" but not "Industry" or "Employee Count").
*Fix:* Audit enrichment configuration: check which fields are mapped in ZoomInfo/Clearbit integration settings. Enable real-time enrichment on new form submissions. Schedule quarterly batch enrichment job for all contacts modified in last 90 days but never enriched.
