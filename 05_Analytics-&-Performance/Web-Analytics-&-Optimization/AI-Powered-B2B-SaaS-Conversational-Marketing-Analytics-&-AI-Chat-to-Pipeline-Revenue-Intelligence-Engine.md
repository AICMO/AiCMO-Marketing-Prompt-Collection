## Success Metrics

**Green light thresholds (program is healthy):**
- Chat-to-meeting rate ≥ 5% of all conversations (or ≥ 25% of ICP-identified conversations)
- Chat-sourced ARR ≥ 8% of new ARR
- Platform ROI ≥ 5x (attributed revenue ÷ platform cost)
- AI bot CSAT ≥ 4.2/5
- ICP identification rate ≥ 30%
- ICP routing accuracy ≥ 90% (ICP accounts routed to live rep within 2 minutes)

**Yellow flags (optimize urgently):**
- Meeting show rate from chat < 65% (indicates qualification quality issue)
- Bot escalation rate > 40% (bot handling too little, or escalation triggers too sensitive)
- Chat-sourced sales cycle longer than average (chat qualification not effective)
- Conversation start rate < 2% of visitors (widget placement or messaging issue)

**Red flags (structural problem):**
- Chat-to-opportunity conversion < 15% of routed conversations
- Platform cost > 20% of attributed revenue
- Negative CSAT trend (3 consecutive months declining)

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Chat-to-pipeline attribution seems inflated — shows 40%+ of pipeline influenced**
Solution: Separate "chat appeared in session before any form fill" from "chat was the primary acquisition channel." Use a strict attribution window: chat must occur before opportunity creation date AND contact must have engaged (≥3 exchanges, not just widget impression). Apply a minimum 30-second engagement threshold to filter accidental triggers.

**Problem: AI bot CSAT is declining but autonomy rate is high**
Solution: High autonomy + declining CSAT = bot is handling conversations it shouldn't. Pull bot conversation transcripts where CSAT ≤ 3 → look for patterns (pricing questions, integration-specific questions, complaint handling). Add these to your escalation triggers immediately. Rebuilding trust with prospects after a poor bot experience is expensive — it's better to over-escalate than under-escalate on high-intent signals.

**Problem: ICP accounts visit but don't start conversations**
Solution: Bot-first flows deter high-value buyers (C-suite, VPs) who expect human response. Run an A/B test: ICP accounts (identified via IP match or account-based targeting) see a "Direct line to [Rep Name]" proactive message vs. generic bot greeting. In most B2B SaaS contexts, personalized proactive messages to known ICP accounts drive 3–4x higher conversation start rates than generic triggers.
