## Overview
This prompt builds a complete messaging architecture for B2B SaaS products with AI agent or autonomous workflow capabilities — the specialized framework PMMs need when their product doesn't just assist humans but increasingly acts *for* them. Use it when you're launching agentic features, autonomous copilots, or AI-driven workflow automation and your existing messaging creates fear (job displacement) or skepticism (AI hype fatigue) instead of urgency and adoption.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: End users are resisting adoption even after purchase — messaging convinced the buyer but not the user**
*Solution:* Your messaging was optimized for economic buyers and landed the deal, but the user-facing narrative is missing. Go back to Deliverable 3 (Hero Narrative) and create a version specifically for end-user internal communications — this means email templates for your champion to forward to their team, an in-product onboarding sequence, and a "30-day impact report" that shows the user their own time savings. The user needs to be the hero in their own story, not a passenger in their manager's decision.

**Problem: "AI replacing jobs" objection keeps derailing deals even after using the reframe**
*Solution:* The objection isn't logical — it's emotional and political. If the reframe isn't working, you need social proof, not better logic. Find 2–3 customers where end users (not just managers) publicly endorse the product. Video testimonials from individual contributors saying "I used to dread Mondays; now I'm doing the work I actually got into this field for" are worth more than 10 executive case studies. If you don't have this proof yet, prioritize collecting it from your happiest users before pushing into enterprise accounts where this objection carries more political weight.

**Problem: Messaging is landing in SMB deals but stalling in enterprise — IT and Legal are blocking**
*Solution:* Your messaging architecture is probably under-indexed on governance and control. Enterprises don't buy autonomous AI — they buy AI *with control architecture*. Go back to Deliverable 4 (Autonomy Trust Spectrum) and make Rung 1–2 the featured entry point in enterprise conversations, not a concession. Build a dedicated "Enterprise Trust Package" — a one-page security brief, a data processing addendum template, an audit log overview, and a governance configuration guide. Position IT not as a gatekeeper you need to get past, but as the co-architect of your implementation. Their buy-in converts them from blocker to champion.
