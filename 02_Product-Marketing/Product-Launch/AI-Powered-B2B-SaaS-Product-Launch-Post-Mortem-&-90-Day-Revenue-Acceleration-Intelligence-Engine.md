## Overview
This prompt drives a systematic post-launch diagnostic for B2B SaaS product marketing teams — converting raw launch performance data into a structured revenue acceleration playbook. Use it 30–60 days after any significant product or feature launch to diagnose what worked, what missed, why pipeline fell short of plan, and exactly what to do in the next 60 days to close the gap.

**Launch Grade: C**

**Primary Failure Mode: Demand Volume (60% of gap), Sales Readiness (30%), Messaging (10%)**

The $510K pipeline shortfall is not a conversion problem — it's a volume problem compounded by a sales articulation gap. 12 demos against a 30-demo goal means the funnel never had enough top-of-funnel to meet revenue targets, regardless of conversion rates.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: "We don't have clean pipeline attribution — campaigns weren't tagged consistently."**
Fix: Use a launch-window proxy method. Pull all new pipeline opened in the 30 days post-launch and compare it to the equivalent 30-day period from the prior quarter and prior year. This gives you a rough-but-defensible baseline delta. Note the methodology limitation explicitly in the Executive Report. Going forward, add this constraint to the prompt: "Attribution data is estimated using launch-window comparison; confidence is medium."

**Problem: "The output gives generic recommendations like 'update your messaging' without specifics."**
Fix: Add verbatim customer language to the objection and messaging fields. The more specific the input ("They said: 'Your AI forecasting isn't better than a CRO who's been doing this for 20 years'"), the more specific the output. Also paste 2–3 lines from actual CRM deal notes or support tickets. Specificity in → specificity out.

**Problem: "The 90-day playbook has 20 plays — we only have 2 people to execute."**
Fix: Add this constraint to the prompt before the Output section: "Our team has 2 PMMs at 30% capacity. Return only the 5 highest-ROI plays totaling no more than 60 person-hours. Rank them by (Impact × Speed) ÷ Effort and state the total estimated person-hours for each." This forces the AI to triage rather than brainstorm.
