## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** Low email open rates (<20%) on launch announcement despite warm customer list
**Solution:** The announcement email may be too product-centric. Rewrite the subject line to lead with the customer's pain (e.g., "Your Q3 reporting just got 18 hours shorter") rather than the feature name. Also check send time — enterprise compliance teams open email Tuesday-Thursday 8-10am in their timezone. Test a plain-text version vs. HTML for enterprise segment.

**Problem:** Feature activated in account but no expansion conversation triggered
**Solution:** Activation alone is insufficient — you need value realization signals. Add a "first meaningful outcome" milestone (e.g., first compliance report completed via the feature) as your expansion trigger instead of simple activation. Configure your product analytics to emit this event to your CRM and re-run the trigger criteria.

**Problem:** CSMs are not following the expansion playbook (low pipeline creation from launch)
**Solution:** CSMs often don't initiate expansion conversations because they fear damaging the relationship. Run a 60-minute CSM enablement session focused on the "success story" framing: "Based on what [similar customer] achieved, I wanted to share an opportunity that might be relevant to your team." Provide a script, not just a slide deck. Gamify with a leaderboard tracking expansion pipeline per CSM.
