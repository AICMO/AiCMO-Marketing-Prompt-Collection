## Overview

Use this when launching a new product, major feature, or pricing tier where partner amplification is critical to achieving ARR targets.

## Troubleshooting

**Issue 1: Partner reps aren't using the enablement assets — low asset download rates**
*Cause:* Asset delivery in email is forgettable; reps don't associate it with revenue opportunity.
*Fix:* Embed the most critical assets (battle card + email templates) directly in the deal registration confirmation email — reps see them at the moment of highest motivation. Add a completion incentive: "$250 SPIF for partner reps who complete the 3-asset launch pack + register a deal by [Date]."

**Issue 2: Partner-sourced pipeline is being created but deals aren't progressing — low velocity**
*Cause:* Partner reps are registering deals before qualification, or they lack access to joint selling support.
*Fix:* Implement a Partner Qualified Deal standard: a deal should only be registered after a discovery call confirming BANT. For stalled deals, offer "Partner Assist" — your SE joins the next call. Create a "Partner Rescue" email template for partner reps to invoke joint selling support within 30 seconds.

**Issue 3: Hyperscaler marketplace launch is delayed — co-sell activation not ready**
*Cause:* Marketplace listing compliance review takes longer than expected; co-sell registration not completed.
*Fix:* Submit marketplace listing and co-sell registration request 45 days before launch (not 30). Build a parallel "soft launch" path where you activate the marketplace listing in a limited region first while approval processes complete for others. Never make marketplace a T-0 dependency — position it as a 30-day post-launch amplification lever.
