## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Journalists aren't responding to launch day pitches.**
Fix: Pivot to the story angle, not the product announcement. Offer exclusive access to a customer willing to be interviewed, or share a proprietary data point that creates a standalone news peg beyond the product itself. Reporters cover trends and data — the product is just proof.

**Problem: Press release sounds like a feature announcement, not a market moment.**
Fix: Remove all bullet points listing features. Rewrite the opening paragraph starting with the market problem or industry stat, not "Company X today announced." The product should appear in paragraph 2. The market moment belongs in paragraph 1.

**Problem: Press coverage generated traffic but zero pipeline.**
Fix: Audit UTM parameters for breaks (most common cause). If tracking is intact, the coverage page lacks a conversion path — add an inline CTA to the blog post or press page before the next launch. Also check: are SDRs actually running the coverage-referencing outreach sequence, or is it sitting in a doc?
