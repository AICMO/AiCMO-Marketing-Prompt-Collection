## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Low email open rates on deprecation announcement**
*Fix:* Ensure the subject line is from a human name (e.g., "From Sarah at Vantara") not a company alias. Suppress from any active promotional email sequences in the same week. Consider a follow-up phone call or direct mail for top 50 enterprise accounts.

**Problem: Enterprise customers are demanding contract extensions beyond the deprecation date**
*Fix:* Create a formal extension policy before you launch (e.g., up to 30-day extension available for accounts >$50K ARR with approved migration plan in place). Never set precedent of indefinite extensions — it signals you're not serious about the deprecation date and slows migration momentum across the board.

**Problem: High support volume overwhelming the team during migration period**
*Fix:* Publish a comprehensive migration FAQ and video walkthrough library before the announcement goes out. Add a chatbot workflow specifically for migration questions. Create a migration-dedicated support alias that routes to a specialized team. Run proactive office hours webinars 2x/week during peak migration periods (Days 60–90) to deflect individual support requests.
