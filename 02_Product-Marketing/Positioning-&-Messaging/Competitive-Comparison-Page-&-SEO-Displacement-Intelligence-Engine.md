## Troubleshooting
- **Problem:** The comparison page reads as biased and salesy, reducing trust.
  **Solution:** Add a genuine "When [Competitor] is better" section with 3 real scenarios. Buyers can smell dishonesty. Pages that acknowledge competitor strengths convert better because they signal confidence. If you're afraid to acknowledge any competitor advantage, your positioning work isn't done yet.

- **Problem:** The page isn't ranking despite good content.
  **Solution:** Bottom-of-funnel comparison pages need authority signals — internal links from your highest-authority pages (homepage, pricing page, category page), and at least 3-5 external backlinks from review sites (G2, Capterra profile links), partner blogs, or industry publications. Submit to Google Search Console immediately after publishing.

- **Problem:** The AI output includes claims we can't substantiate (e.g., exact G2 ratings, specific customer counts).
  **Solution:** All statistics, ratings, and customer quotes are placeholders that must be verified before publishing. Replace bracket-enclosed numbers with real data from your G2 profile, internal CRM, and customer success team. Publishing unverified claims creates legal and credibility risk.
