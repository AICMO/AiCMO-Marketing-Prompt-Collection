## Integration Tips

- **HubSpot / Salesforce:** Pull deal stage drop-off rates and common objection disposition tags to populate the Conversion Tax module inputs before running the audit
- **Gong / Chorus / Salesloft:** Export the top 20 objection themes from call recordings; paste into the "common objections" input field for the most precise Objection Pre-emption Audit
- **Google Optimize / Optimizely / VWO:** Use the Quick Wins section output as direct test briefs — each rewrite is structured to feed directly into an A/B test setup
- **Notion / Confluence:** Store the Messaging Audit Report as a living document; re-run the audit quarterly or after any major product launch, pricing change, or competitive shift
- **Figma / Webflow:** Send the Quick Wins rewrites directly to your design team as annotated copy-change tickets with before/after and conversion hypothesis embedded
- **Slack automation:** Set a monthly recurring reminder to re-run Module 3 (Differentiation Sharpness) as a standing competitive messaging check — competitors update messaging faster than most teams notice

## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** The audit output is too generic and doesn't feel specific to my company.
**Solution:** The more verbatim copy you paste in (not summaries), the more precise the audit. Paste exact ad headlines, exact email subject lines, and exact sales deck slide text — the AI diagnoses specific words, not categories.

**Problem:** I don't have funnel conversion data to populate the Conversion Tax module.
**Solution:** Use directional estimates: even rough benchmarks (e.g., "our demo page converts around 8%") are enough to calculate relative opportunity. If you have zero data, describe qualitative observations: "Sales says most deals stall after the demo" — the AI will map that to the BOFU stage.

**Problem:** The recommended rewrites don't match our brand voice.
**Solution:** Add a brand voice constraint in the INPUTS section: "Brand voice: direct, no jargon, never uses the word 'leverage' or 'synergy', approachable-expert tone similar to Linear or Notion." The AI will recalibrate all rewrites to that constraint.
