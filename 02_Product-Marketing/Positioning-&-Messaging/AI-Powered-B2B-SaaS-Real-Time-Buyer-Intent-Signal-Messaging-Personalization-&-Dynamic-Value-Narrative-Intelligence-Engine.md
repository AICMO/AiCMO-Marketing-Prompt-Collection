## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Intent signal data is noisy — prospects flagged as "high-intent" don't actually convert at higher rates**
Solution: Audit your signal weighting. Third-party Bombora/G2 intent alone is weak without first-party confirmation. Require at least ONE first-party signal (website visit, content download, email click) to compound with third-party data before activating high-urgency sequences. Build a 60-day lookback window and compare intent-activated conversion rates vs. control to identify which specific signals actually predict pipeline — many marketers find only 2-3 signal combinations actually move the needle.

**Problem: Can't build dynamic content in current MAP — only static segments**
Solution: Start with macro-segmentation rather than signal-level personalization. Create 3-4 broad segments (e.g., "Competitive Evaluator," "Funding Trigger," "Champion Re-engagement," "Cold Inbound") and build separate email sequences for each. Use CRM workflow rules to assign new contacts to a segment on creation based on available data. This delivers 70% of the personalization lift with 20% of the technical complexity — then upgrade to dynamic content once you've proven ROI.

**Problem: SDRs aren't using the personalized messaging — defaulting to generic scripts**
Solution: The issue is usually that signal context isn't surfaced in the CRM in a human-readable format. Instead of just storing "Intent Score: 8," create a CRM field called "Why to Call This Account Now" that auto-populates with a plain-English summary: "This account is actively researching CLM alternatives on G2, visited your pricing page twice this week, and just posted a VP Legal Ops job — making them a top priority." When reps see this, they use it. Build this with a simple workflow that concatenates signal fields into a human-readable text field.
