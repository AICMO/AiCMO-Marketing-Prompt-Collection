## Overview

Use it when launching a new product, entering a new market, rebuilding your ICP, or scaling a personalization program that needs to work across email, ads, SDR outreach, and website content simultaneously.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Output feels generic — could apply to any product in the category.**
Fix: Add 3-5 specific customer quotes (verbatim, even rough paraphrases) to the prompt under each persona. Real language from win/loss interviews anchors the AI to your specific buyers' vocabulary. Also specify the top 2-3 competitors by name and what you know about why you win/lose against them.

**Problem: Messaging matrix has too many personas and becomes unmanageable.**
Fix: Limit to 3 primary personas per run. If you have 6+ personas, segment by deal type (SMB vs. Enterprise vs. Mid-Market) and run separate matrices for each. The most effective companies have 2-3 personas who cover 80% of revenue — identify those and start there.

**Problem: SDRs aren't using the matrix — they default to generic outreach.**
Fix: The matrix is too abstract until it's translated into specific sequences. Use the "Channel-Specific Copy Variants" section (Section 4) to build sequence templates directly in your sequencing tool (Outreach, Salesloft, Apollo). Reduce the matrix to a 1-page "Persona Cheat Sheet" with 3 subject lines, 1 opening line, and 3 objection rebuttals per persona — that's what SDRs actually use.
